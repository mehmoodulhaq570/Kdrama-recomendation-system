Firstclass is the first student to earn straight A's at the Faculty of Law in the last decade. He is very serious and strict when it comes to punctuality, which can be seen from the fact that one of his hobbies is collecting watches and clocks. Minute is a self-centered, spoiled rich girl whose world crashes when her family files for bankruptcy. To survive (and avoid living in a squalid room), she pretends to be Firstclass's gay brother's girlfriend. She is paid a monthly stipend to live in their apartment. However, it seems that Minute and Firstclass cannot stand being in each other's presence without fighting. How can they get along? Edit Translation

I wasn't sure what to expect from this instalment of the U-Prince series but I decided to give it a go anyway.

The drama is only 4 episodes which I didnt realise until i started watching. The story is literally just about a spoilt rich girl who's parents want to teach her a lesson and whilst I don't agree with the way they did it I think it's a good lesson. What I didn't like was how easily the word "rape" is used and then there's that silly sound effect straight after. I didn't think it was appropriate.

I love the chemistry that the 2 leads have. It's very sweet and innocent in my opinion. I also enjoyed watching the female lead with her best friend - especially as he seemed to know exactly where she was - without making it sound stalky. :D

The OST didn't really grab my attention.

Whilst there are cute and funny scenes I don't see myself rewatching the whole drama, just the couple of scenes that I enjoyed.

I recommend this drama for those who are looking for something extremely short and sweet to watch in between longer dramas.
