A passing comet with mysterious powers brings about wonderful human relationships. A dog owner and a cat owner start to exhibit animalistic traits whenever they're unhappy, yet their unusual ordeal enables them to discover the true meaning of happiness. When Comet WTF666 chose the earth as its target and its pinkish tail swept around the world, it meant to spread happiness and love to humanity. Miao Yu is a tacky CEO with an entrepreneurial mind. Wang Chen is an introverted designer who doesn't mix well within fashion circles. Their fates become intertwined because of a livestream and an accident at the pet hospital. From then on, once they feel unhappy, Miao Yu starts acting like Wang Chen's cat while Wang Chen starts acting like Miao Yu's dog. It's an arrangement that brings occasional surprises and distresses. Honestly, what a legit show. THE CHEMISTRY AND KISSES ARE EVERYTHING. And I couldn't help but crack up. I just got my wisdom teeth out and this was really binge worthy but I took my time and it was SO WORTH IT. I really needed a filler show and this was where it was at. It seemed a little odd and far-fetched in the beginning but if you really give it a chance, it's actually such a cute and legit show. Like, WHAT IS THIS COMET? LMAO. Everything kind of moves a little fast to so things are just happening in the blink of an eye but it's such an easy watch. You have two people that meet by fate, honestly. Things just really bring them together and not only that but friendship comes together as well and honestly, the rest is history. It has your fashion, it has your ups and downs and it has a lot going on definitely but in reality, it's just a pure good show.

I loved the ML and FL, they were cute, it was fluffy, their chemistry was just fantastic right off the bat and they really clicked. I love the co-workers, I love the friends, I love the families and even the exes aren't even too bad, annoying, but not bad at all.

The music is great!!!

Overall, I think I would rewatch this show. Lol. The two main leads make the show, they're cute, they match, it's fluffy, they're honestly really sweet and even in the moments that they tried to make mellow and dramatic, they really just worked out and worked well together. I loved all the characters and what they played, I loved the ups and downs. This show is honestly just so very cute. So 10/10 for me, if you need a filler show when you have nothing better to do and you really just want some romance in your life, this is where it is at.

I am new in getting into Chinese dramas as of late but this Rom-com is definitely one of the best that I've watched, causing me to binge watch a majority of the episodes, even downloading the free app to catch up! The 'iQLYL' app I used to watch has ENG subs but keep in mind they are kind of worded odd but still understandable.

TL;DR - All in all this is a simple fluffy, heartwarming rom-com that has more to give the more you watch. If you are looking for romance that's sweet, cute, has just the right amount of comedy and is practically angst free (featuring cute animals), then this is the drama for you!

Although the plot seems childish and weird (the comet name is literally WTF 666... lol) the drama definitely has a lot more heart and love to it in such a pure and endearing way. Like how the leads develop their companionship/friendship/relationship and help each other grow as adult individuals. Being able to see and feel the growth of the main leads was really heartwarming. I can't tell you how much I enjoyed this series. The chemistry between all the characters work so well together, especially the main leads (the kisses!! The FL is so cute). Even all the side/support characters won me over by the end in some way. The story although sometimes predictable is done well enough to the point where you actually anticipate to see something happen. Another positive is that the pace of the drama was great. Although some plot lines are briefly mentioned and shelved for later episodes, difficult/hindering situations never drag on for too long.

I do think the series could have been cut by an episode or two story wise but the more sweet moments they give me I will gladly take! Something I think this drama could've done better is finding a better way to portray the "Cat" personality but once watching, I guess I can understand how it's difficult to portray and how each animal has their own unique personality. There are plot holes here and there but none big enough (imo) that break the reality, just small things like, "no way you can get a taxi that fast".
