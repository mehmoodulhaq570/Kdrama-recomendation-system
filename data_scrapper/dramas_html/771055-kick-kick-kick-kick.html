Ji Jin Hui used to be a popular actor and entertainer. He founded the production company Kick Kick Kick Kick Company with Cho Yeong Sik. Jo Young Sik was once a star PD. A scandal ended his heydays as a PD. Even though he didn't have a good relationship with Ji Jin Hui at that time, they started Kick Kick Kick Kick Company together and have run the company together. They have employees, including Baek Ji Won, who works as a therapist for employees and Lee Min Jae, who is a workaholic and determined to find success. (Source: AsianWiki) Edit Translation

A cringey, fun, dad humor, show.

Since this show has no review, I decided to write one. This show was my first drama in the sitcom genre. Personally, it's not for me, but nevertheless, I liked it since I watched it with my family, and it genuinely has funny parts sometimes. It's just like those Selena Gomez, Hannah Montana-type Disney kids shows, with quick jokes in between monologues and random characters doing things for no apparent reason. Give it a try if you are into that. The only difference from a Disney kids' show is that in this show, something or the other is constantly happening on the screen; it gives you no room to breathe, and my God, the shaky cam makes it difficult to pay attention sometimes. I watched it for Ji Jin-hee, 7.5/10.

If you like Welcome to Waikiki (Eulachacha Waikiki), you'll love this!

My opinion, this series absolutely hilarious and easy-to-watch, it's been a long time since I've enjoyed a comedy k-drama. It's giving the same vibe as Waikiki because it's setting in workplace, leans more towards bromance/friendship (found family), have multiple love interest and comforting characters! both main leads also have the opposites-attract (grumpy x drama queen) but still work out as a team. The support roles also cute (i adores them so much). the main points is just try giving it a shot (i find the very 2 episodes boring and dumb but it's getting better and better in episode 3!). I'm dare to say all the male characters are green flags when it's comes to relationship (I REALLY LOVE KANG PD AND SCREENWRITER WANG LOVE STORY).

Last but not least, this show taught me to get to know more people before you judge them, my introvert side will never match their energy and personality but once i adapt and accept it, they're very loud (maybe some of you will find their characters annoying) but they all have very warm heart!! i'm very relatedtable with lee minjae as an ISTJ need to work with loud and crazy people everyday.
