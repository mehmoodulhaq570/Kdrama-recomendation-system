Workplace rookie Wang Cui Hua unexpectedly finds herself inside the world of a novel, where she crosses paths with Zhang San, another transmigrator—this time, a hidden king who has been biding his time for a decade. One struggling against fate, the other concealing power behind a façade, the two join forces to survive. As schemes unfold and alliances shift, they must face not only the dangers within the story but also the cruel prophecy that one of them will not live to see the flourishing world they’ve worked so hard to build. (Source: MyDramaList) ~~ Adapted from the web novel "This Is Ridiculous" (成何体统) by Qi Ying Jun (七英俊). Edit Translation
