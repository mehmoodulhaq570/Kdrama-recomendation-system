Fluffy friendship drama with ample heatthrobbing moments!

This is underrated on mdl in my opinion

The most they did in the story was hold hands but it was full of heatthrobbing moments throughout the story. Acting was so spot on from all the characters. Everyone was convincing enough to pull me into their school/class. Very minutely expressed emotions can be seen through out the plot. Love triangles, childish schemes, jealousy, anxiety, empathy, etc. all emotions were depicted very carefully. I remember shouting "so cute" at many characters in every episode. All of their actions are just so cute and fulfilling.

If you are looking for a light and fluffy drama with cotton candy moments melting in your mouth, this is a drama you should give a try.

My Deskmate (2021) is a fun and entertaining series overall

The story is simple and easy to follow, focusing on everyday school life, filled with light comedy, friendship, and sweet moments.

The female lead’s acting may seem inexperienced at first, but that raw and natural style actually became one of the charming points of the series. It made her character feel more real and relatable.

If you enjoy school dramas with a slice-of-life feel and lighthearted storytelling, My Deskmate is definitely worth checking out.
