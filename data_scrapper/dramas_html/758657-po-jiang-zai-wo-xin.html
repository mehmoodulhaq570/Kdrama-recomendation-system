But the Heavens gave her another chance by granting her rebirth. She will use this opportunity to get revenge. This is when she meets Fu Yun Lan, a "pilot of the stars" who, like her, hides a lot of secrets... (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

Lmao what a sh!tty ending

First of all, the male lead in this series is one of the most attractive men I've ever seen in my life. I need him in ways that are not appropriate to say in this review.

Anyway, this show was garbage, not exactly the fun kind. Fortunately it's short, but as a whole it doesn't work in the least. The story is ridiculously skimpy, a lot of things are left up in the air and unexplained, the characters act like they have no brains but three scripted dialogues written by a poorly paid person.

And then we have that ending that pretends to be poetic when it's just moronic and ridiculous. It reminds me a bit of the ending of The Rainbow in Our Memory but that was a good drama, one of the best C-Dramas I've seen btw. Instead here the outcome is anticlimactic. Far from being emotional it made me laugh because of how stupid it was. No doubt the writers had no idea how to end this garbage and came up with the most ridiculous closure ever.

Anyway Dai Gao Zheng i need you so bad.
