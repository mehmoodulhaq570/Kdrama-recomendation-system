If that was the ending then NO

***Spoilers***
I did not invest all that time and emotion in these characters for the writers to treat us as test subjects. If this is really the end then I won’t watch anything else they write. Ever. We followed this show and these characters through two seasons and deserved a better ending for all. Some did deserve to find happiness and some obviously did not. The ghost plot was dumb and I ignored it through most of the series anyway. I’ve been disappointed in endings before but nothing like this. It seems the writers went out of their way to shock us unnecessarily.
