On the eve of school graduation, Ruan Yu met Teng Yi because of her part-time tutoring. Ruan Yu is eager to take root in the big city, and Teng Yi is eager to escape the constraints and arrangements of his family. They accidentally broke into each other's world, enlightened each other, and attracted each other, but because Ruan Yu suddenly suffered a family change, the relationship between the two came to an abrupt end. Four years later, Ruan Yu stayed at the school to work and lived a stable life. Teng Yi returned to China alone to found an outdoor products company. The two met again because of the college sports league. In the cooperation, they each found a new direction for their careers. Ruan Yu regained her dream and went abroad for further studies. Four years later, the two met again when they attended an industry summit forum. The two decided not to miss each other after a long separation and bravely ran for love, finally drawing a perfect end to this eight-year relationship.
