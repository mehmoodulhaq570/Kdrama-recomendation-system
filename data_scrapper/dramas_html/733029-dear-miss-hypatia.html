On the first day of reporting to the university, Jian Dan, a village girl from Shenmu Village, accidentally pushed Han Yan Sheng , the president of the swimming club, into the swimming pool. Because he could not successfully obtain credits for club activities, Jian Dan could not obtain a scholarship on campus, so he had to start his rock climbing club. However, on campus, the more Jian Dan wanted to keep a distance from Han Yan Sheng, the more fate played tricks on Jian Dan, and their common hobby, "mathematics," made them more closely connected. By chance, Han Yan Sheng discovered that "Hypatia", who matched his interest in mathematics, was Jian Dan. At the same time, Jian Dan discovered the secret behind Han Yan Sheng's inability to swim, which also touched on the painful memories that Han Yansheng had been hiding in his heart... Fun and cute youth drama

Love Formula is a drama to watch when you just want to sit, relax and laugh. This drama is based from the web novel Master Devi Do Not Kiss Me so I knew what to expect from the beginning. If you want to enjoy this type of old fashioned story do not over think every little detail. This is not meant to be a serious type of drama. If you can’t do this, then this might not be the drama that you’re looking for. The rating for this drama isn’t very high, but do watch for yourself to decide if it’s worth your time or not.

Let’s start with the good things this drama has to offer.
* Song YiRen never disappoints with her acting skills. She played the role of Jian Dian very well! She gets a 10! I’m not familiar with the rest of the cast, but they did okay (that’s why I gave the acting and cast an 8).
* There are very cute, sweet, and funny moments between the leads.
****Spoiler ahead**** Who knew that the male lead could accidentally trip and end up doing a Spider-Man type of upside down kiss while trying to save the female lead from falling while rock climbing, lol.
* I really enjoyed that this drama is about friendship and not just romance.
* The second CP is so sweet and the third CP is really funny.
* There are a lot of misunderstandings, but they are solved very quickly and don’t drag on for a long time.
*The OST is good.

Now the things I disliked about this drama.
* We had two mean and annoying girls that liked the male lead. Was one not enough?
* The mean girls got away with everything and there were no consequences. Luckily one girl stopped making trouble while the other went on with her scheming.
* The male lead could’ve been better at communicating with female lead.

Before watching, keep in mind that there will be frustrating moments, but there will also be moments that will make you laugh. The funny and cute moments make Love Formula worth checking out.
