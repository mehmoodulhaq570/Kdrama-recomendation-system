Suffering from a separation anxiety disorder, Kang Pa Rang confesses his love for someone different every week. Lee Shi Won, Pa Rang's headstrong best friend, has stopped Pa Rang from dating anyone for the past 10 years. After getting rejected by girls time after time, Pa Rang confesses his love for Shi Won, his friend of 10 years, but gets rejected. There is a reason there are weird rumors about Shi Eon, the psychopath that is socially awkward but was elected class president for 10 consecutive years. He hides his true feelings behind his cold comments, but there is something in the way he looks at his decade-old friend Shi Won. At 18 years old, they have faced many hardships, challenges, and confessions, but it often fails. It’s an awkward age. Will they be able to find love instead of just having it end as an attempt? It's as it is title suggests Failing in Love not falling in love.

It was a good watch for me, I enjoyed it and specially the ost was amazing it's one of best part of this web drama. As it's name suggest it's about failure in the love lives of teenagers, their feelings, struggles, emotions, confusions, finding what they want from life and the friendship between them. It's a pretty much portrayal of a regular teenagers life I can understand cause I'm passing through same phase.

The execution is pretty much realistic, I liked the fact that even though it's just a short drama we get to know the backstories of characters and also pretty much big character development in the end. Although the ending wasn't fairytale like they lived happily ever after together but I really liked that it wasn't typical but everyone was happy and satisfied in there own life in own way, it was perfect ending for me.

This isn't about who ends up together or who gets the girl it's much more than that.

Okay so after finishing this drama I have mixed feelings about this. What I most like of FAIling in Love is the cinematography and the OST, a few times i was like 'this feels like i'm watching a movie'. Also the production was good. But, and here is one of my problems, I wanna talk about Pa Rang, because at first it was the typical character you may like because is cute and innocent. But after seeing a few chapters my opinion change and i was like 'oh I don't like him, he's so unmature' but after knew his story I fell in love again, and then at the final I found it childish. He's my favourite character, after all I think he's a really good person, innocent and he doesn't have any bad thinkings. He's pure and the most real (even though his weird behavior). But, after a few mixed feelings about the characters, it was a really good drama, with an amazing script. I recommend it because it's so easy to watch, you will be involved with all the characters and the story, really, they did a very good job. At first I was insecure to watch it because it doesn't catched my attention, but thank god i didn't pass and i finally decided to watch it, because it was a big surprise. I hope to see more works of any of them ^^ I recommend it a lot! (sorry if i have a grammatical errors, english is not my native language)
