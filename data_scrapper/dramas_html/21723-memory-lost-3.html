This review may contain spoilers

It is an amazing drama. TOTALLY RECOMMENDED.

I will now give my final judgement for this drama

If you have read my previous reviews of the other seasons, you'll notice that my rating for this drama became higher because it met my expectations properly.

The story was filled with a lot of plot holes and in the process of trying to give us suspense, the author may have gotten a tad bit too excited but it wasn't anything bad enough to ruin the drama. In fact, it made the drama much more interesting for me. the story is really good and we don't expect any less from the writer of love me if you dare and when a snail falls in love.

- ACTORS/ACTING/CHARACTERS
The female lead was a good character. I think Yang Rong is a good actress. It's obvious the writer wanted her to be smart and intuitive but also have a soft and sassy side. I noticed that her sassiness annoyed people but I think that is where most of her charm came from. She was also quite talkative like me. I felt like I could relate so much with her character because it oddly reminded me of myself. Her relationship with the male lead was also amazing. (PS: you'll get used to the heels eventually, it's like a super power).

The male lead had a very masculine charm to himself. I absolutely hated Johnny Bai mainly because of his character in Love O2O, more reason why I didn't want to watch this drama but I did fall in love with him eventually. his character as Han Chen was really good. Although he is tease of a man and a big headed prick, he is very loyal not only to his lover and friends but also to his job. He's an upright man and quite the family man too. Totally husband material.

Now lets talk about the most controversial character in this story Xu Si Bai also known as S ( I personally call him doctor forensic chef guy). from the onset of the drama, he already gave us very off vibes. All our suspicions were eventually proven to be right and even expanded as he just happened to be the founder of the most psychotic serial killing group ever. Towards the end however, my animosity for him changed to a sort of attraction. I found him way more attractive when he was in serial killer S mode than when he was doctor forensic chef guy. I never wanted him close to the female lead, but the SML syndrome hit right at the end of the drama.

I absolutely loved the alphabet group. Each of them were amazing in their own way, the actors were really good especially Zhao Yi Qin who took the role of M. I was particularly attached to each of them and I even came up with cute villain names for them. (The Artist, Bomb boy, chemical guy, child bride, professor mind games, hacker and Sniper... lets not forget their leader Doctor forensic chef guy) LOL.

The other characters were good too. Cold face, little hacker and the other cute guy were really nice characters that you'll grow to love so much. Some people felt like cold faces relationship with the little nurse was out of place but I think it was okay, they were quite cute.

All in all, the characters were good. the chemistry between the leads was impeccable, the Alphabet group gave me the thrill I was looking for and the cute police officer friends were worth loving.

-MUSIC
there wasn't much to the music, I don't think they wanted us to concentrate on it anyways

- REWATCH
most likely, probably years later when I've forgotten most of it and need a good dose of love and crime.

All in all it was a good drama, a total recommendation. I think it will satisfy the needs of those looking for action and also those looking for a sweet romance.
