During his PhD studies, Shu Ren falls in love with his junior, Sun Shu. However, family crises cause Shu Ren to drop out without telling Sun Shu, who leaves for Japan to study. Five years later, they reunite at Si Kang Hospital, where Shu Ren seeks reconciliation, but Sun Shu coldly rejects him. As Sun Shu’s mother falls ill, their bond reignites. Shu Ren struggles with guilt over his mother's past death, but Sun Shu helps him heal and regain his confidence. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

So this is my very first Chinese drama that I have watched successfully and to be honestly, I did not think that it's going to be that great.

Let me start by saying that this drama, was realistic in many ways and had its sense of seriousness while dealing with multiple problems. Some of you might have problems with the amount of time in which psychological problems could be solved, but I give it to the directors that they need to complete these problems within certain episodes, thus things got resolved much quickly.

Now let me move on to the actors.

Yan Shuren our main Lead, he is such an amazing actor. Every frame was just built for him. He is calm, composed, practical and when his time comes to really act, during the end of the series, he outshines all.

Sunshu- now I am a little confused by her way of life. As a psychologist, she needs to remain calm but at a lot of places, I found her to be childish. Even the dialogues or the way you portray them, she was at time way to harsh. Well that's what I felt.

Chi fan- Hehe I found him adorable. He is an amazing friend and can I point out. I am so envious of his Marvel collections. Hehehe ????????????

The rest of the supporting characters are amazing too. They'd start to grow on you.

As for the romance part, it's somewhere in the background. Not on the forefront. You'll enjoy the subtle ongoing of the drama for it sure is Melodrama. One needs to have patience to compete this.

For all the episodes I saw, yes I'll recommend you guys can watch this drama, for if you like the calming effect and not too fast paced dramas.

Njoy!!!

I’ll be the odd ball again and say this one is hard pill to swallow. I personally was/am having a hard time connecting with any of the characters. Although they appear to be people with internal struggles it still just feels like it’s missing something. Dumped by your love years ago, meets him again, gives cold shoulder and then all of a sudden friends again. The level of pain displayed does not match with the ease into it again. So far there is 70% hospital drama, 10% family issues and 20% romance/in my feelings. Makes for a good hospital drama but not so much romance drama for me. Acting is good but somethings missing for me, maybe emotions. Had to drop it.
