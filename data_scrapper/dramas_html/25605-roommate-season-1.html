The real person behind the celebrity

For me this was a foreign language show and I only knew three out of the eleven celebrities including chanyeol, Lee dong wook and kang joon. Honestly, I picked to watch it because of chanyeol. As I began watching the first episode and got to know all characters I was hooked. It was interesting to see all these people from different backgrounds and their natural style, how they are strangers at the start and then slowly get comfortable with each other. There are plenty of laughs and enjoyable moments and while watching this you will feel yourself become a part of their family. I started to like nana, bom and soo hyun. The seniors like shin sung woo and Lee dong wook really earned my respect. We get to their personal stories and background and it's really interesting to see these lovable kind and caring people. They truly won my heart.

"Roommate Season 1 (2014): A Hilarious and Heartwarming Peek into Unlikely Bonds"


"Roommate Season 1 (2014)" is a Korean reality show that brings together a diverse group of celebrities in a shared living environment. The series offers an inside look into the daily lives of these public figures as they navigate living together, showcasing their interactions, friendships, and challenges.

The show's strength lies in its varied cast, featuring actors, musicians, comedians, and other entertainers. The diverse mix of personalities creates an engaging dynamic that leads to both entertaining and heartwarming moments.

Throughout the season, viewers get a glimpse into the celebrities' lives as they perform household tasks, engage in activities, and bond with one another. The unscripted nature of the show allows for genuine and relatable moments, offering a more humanizing perspective of these stars beyond their glamorous images.

While the interactions and friendships among the cast members provide entertaining content, there are moments where the pacing feels slow or lacking in significant events, impacting the overall engagement of certain episodes.

Despite occasional lulls, "Roommate Season 1" remains an enjoyable watch for those interested in the behind-the-scenes lives of Korean celebrities and their interactions in a shared living space.
