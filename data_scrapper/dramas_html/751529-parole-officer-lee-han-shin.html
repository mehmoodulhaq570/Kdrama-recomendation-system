Lee Han Sin, a former lawyer turned parole examiner, is dedicated to preventing inmates from exploiting the parole system through money, connections, or deceit—however, he's compassionate to people who are unjustly imprisoned, helping them whenever possible. Han Sin teams up with ace detective An Seo Yun and an unexpected ally, legendary loan shark Choi Hwa Ran, with the ultimate goal of confronting Ji Myeong Seop, a second-generation family business heir who believes he's above the law. Can this unlikely group succeed in its battle against corruption? (Source: Rakuten Viki) Edit Translation

Great gem! Worth the watch

This drama is entertaining, mix of fun, drama and suspense. I don't mind that there's no romance, there are several scenes/eps that thugs the heart and some cutesy scenes. This drama has great rewatch value. If you are looking for something unique, entertaining redemption type of drama, then this is for you. I closely watched per scene and never skipped because I am invested in the story. Great storytelling. Great chemistry among the leads. I truly look forward to season 2.

Amazing Trio!

Who'd have thought we needed a combo of parole office, detective and loan shark?!

I mean the storyline isn't that new, especially if you've watched a lot of similar show- you kinda expect the same pattern of corruption, media manipulation, etc. However, the EXECUTION is sooo refreshing.

As a start, I don't even know parole examiner exists- so it's a nice introduction to learn about new character. And a female loan shark boss- oh hell yea! This is probably one of the most iconic role Baek Ji Won has played! She totally nailed it! I especially love the ending, especially for Hwa Ran

The show is entertaining, funny, and thrilling all at same time! Watch the trailer, or maybe 1st 10 mins of episode 1 and judge it for yourself instead of taking my words or MDL rating. Have fun!
