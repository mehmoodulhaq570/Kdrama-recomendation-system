OMG was that bad and unnecessary

While the series was bad, this was even worse.

But the story was just a fever dream and cringe af. They did try to take a book from the thai adaptation to give us fan-service, but it failed. The leads don't have much chemistry. I was just thinking the whole time "WTF?".

There is not much to say, besides, it was trash and a waste of money (to film) and a waste of my time.
