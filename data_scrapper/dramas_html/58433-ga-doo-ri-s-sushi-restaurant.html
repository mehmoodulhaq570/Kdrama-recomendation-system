A soft, easy watch

This isn't the sort of drama you go into expecting a whole lot in terms of plot, but for a light, quick watch that makes you smile most of the way through, this is just the thing! I started watching an episode of Ga Du Ri's Restaurant after episodes of another, heavier drama so I could get a bit of a break, and it was the perfect cleanser.

It was nice to watch a web series that was longer than 10 minutes but that didn't require a full hour of dragging plot, as so many light filler dramas do. The acting was fine (I hadn't read up on it before watching and was delighted to see Hyuk's cute smile) but they could have done a lot more with the music... please, more than one memorable song on the OST next time!

At only 20 minutes per episode, I can already see this being a comfort re-watch for me.
