Min Yoo Jung is a 25-year-old woman who dreams of becoming a successful pop musician. But her musical dreams are a long way from coming true – and she spends her evenings busking on the streets. Life takes a turn for the weird when Park Yeon, a talented musician from the past is transported from the Joseon Dynasty (1392-1910) to the present day, where he meets Min Yoo Jung and indicates he is ready to help her on her musical quest. However, he is not the only time-traveling visitor to drop in on her: A gifted vocalist by the name of Lee Won also travels from the distant past and makes her acquaintance. And things get even more complicated when Jung Do Young – an entertainment industry professional – uses her own powers to connect with the past to learn of Park Yeon’s secrets… (Source: Viki) ~~ Adapted from the webtoon "It's Thorny" (가시리잇고) by Park Sun Jae (박선재) and Ra Hee (라희). I like it more than the manhwa!

I actually have a mix feeling about this drama, because i found first six eps exciting, but the last two eps are kinda rushed so i dont feel the excitement. Although i feel the ending is kinda rushed im still happy that they dont follow the original ending because the manhwa ending was so tragic i dont think i can bear it.

Story: I have read the manhwa before and i like the webdrama version better. The webdrama is more comedic while the manhwa is more melo and i found it a bit depressing. The premise is good, but i think they could do more. They are too focused on the main lead, they dont do much on the second male lead and second female lead, so we cant sympathise with them and cant see how their characters develop.

Acting and character: This is the first time i watched a drama with more historical story on it so i dont really know how acting on historical should be, but i dont think the acting is bad.. but its not impressive either.
I love the female lead’s acting throughout drama but im slightly not feeling the emotion at the end of the drama when shes parting with the male lead.
Ah and they shouldnt make lee won younger than park yeon because its obvious that lee won is older than park yeon.

Music: i love the music! I love the ost and the song the female lead always sing. I cant get it out of my head lol

Overall its a fun webdrama to watch, the drama is aesthetically pleasing so i enjoy watching it and it only takes approx 4 hours to watch the entire drama!
