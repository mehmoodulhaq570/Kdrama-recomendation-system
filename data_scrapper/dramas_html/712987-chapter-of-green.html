It's cringe but I keep watching it :)

In my dictionary, there are 2 types of cringe, the disgusting cringe and the funny cringe. This series fortunately is the funny cringe type. Even if it's cringing it will keep your eyes on until the last ep cause it's fun to watch.

STORY
As expected from mini series, the story is lacking. There's no connection between ep, the plot is messy, which could be confusing for some people. I know they want to portray how best friend could become a lover because they spend too much time together (from work, play, etc) but how they deliver it through a story is still lacking soo much. There's no such thing as feelings development whatsoever, it's a complete mess. In such a short duration they also try hard to add a conflict but it goes wrong and becomes the weirdest ep of this series (EP 10).

ACTING
I'd say their acting skills aren't great, just okay. Even so, it's still enjoyable.

SONG
They used so many korean songs and I love it lol. Since I know a lot of these songs, I could say they used them at the right time and moment. Their choice of songs is one of the biggest factors this drama is fun to watch imo.

Overall, it's just a so-so series but not bad to watch for those who are not patient enough to wait for The Tuxedo. It's also fun to watch which could make your mood better (that works for me). It's just 12 eps with 1 min duration for each ep so why not give it a go?
