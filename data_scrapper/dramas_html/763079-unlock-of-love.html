Good message, not so good delivery

"Every disappointment leaves scars and endless pain. Endings depend on you. You can lock your heart and let the pain linger or allow someone to break down your wall and unlock your heart."

Love and Rain were SO FRICKIN ADORABLEE, I love how open they were with each other, including physically. I loved even more how they asked their friends and Love's mum for support when they were struggling with their sex lives, it was very open, honest, and healthy, which was really nice to see.
Love's relationship with her mum was very refreshing, they were mother and daughter, sisters, and friends, her mum was her N⁰1 supporter, and she her mothers.

Can't believe they pushed through with Pat/Dean, like my baby Pat was WAYYY too good for Dean, and I felt all of Dean's actions when she was making amends for the past lacked sincerity. Also, what was even so difficult about being truthful and clearing up the misunderstandings? Its not like Dean had some huge secret that if she was honeat about it, their life would never be the same again. She literally created a problem out of nothing.
Wish they would have introduced another character truly deserving and worthy of Pat's love.

All in all, a good but forgettable watch.
I was really enjoying this at one point, but personally, i felt Dean's character single-handedly ruined it.
