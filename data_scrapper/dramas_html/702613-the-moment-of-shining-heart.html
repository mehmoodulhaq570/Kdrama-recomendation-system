Good Enough

Korean Drama "The Moment the Heart Shines" is a school drama with a social media twist.

The drama starts strongly, with a critical view on the social media fever and how it constructs human relationships nowadays. The drama does a great job when it comes to that aspect of the story, especially in the way they handled the friendships.

However, the drama started getting predictable halfway through. The love triangle was predictable and cheesy, and left the second couple half baked as a result. The romance was cliche as well. Thus, the drama got just like all those other school romance dramas out there and lost its unique charm.

Finally, the performances were pretty enjoyable by everyone in the cast.

So, overall, 5 out of 10.
