This review may contain spoilers
This drama was hands down one of the best dramas I have ever watched. I love the cast's chemistry and everyone worked so well with each other. So starting off with the basics; the storyline.
→ Story: (9.5) The storyline was very, very good. Unlike the first season, there was a storyline that kind of followed through throughout the drama, like Mina and Mingyu's complicated love story. However, they managed to make it so non frustrating that I didn't really get too upset. I love the storyline so much and the way that this was wrote out. This series honestly couldn't get any better, both seasons are so amazing and well thought out. Maybe the one thing I didn't like was actually just Mina and Mingyu's relationship before they got together. It had me screaming at my screen,"JUST GET TOGETHER, YOU GUYS KNOW YOU LIKE EACH OTHER!"
→ Acting/Cast: (10) Whoever casted the actors need an award because they did so amazing at choosing actors. Everyone did what they had to, everyone did their parts, and they also did it WELL. No one disappointed, everyone portrayed their roles perfectly, I will miss this cast so so much :(
→ Music: (10) I don't really listen to music but I noticed that they used a song that was used in Season 1, which actually contributed to it the overall, since it made me nostalgic.
→ Rewatch value: (9.5) I love this drama as a whole so I know for sure I will come back to it but the reason why I took .5 off was because I just didn't like sitting through episode after episode of Mina and Mingyu ignoring each other when it was so obvious that they cared so much about each other.
→ Overall: (10) Overall, THIS DRAMA IS THE BEST AND I LOVE IT SO MUCH OH MY GOD. I love both seasons and personally, I prefer Season 1 but both seasons are SO SO good and deserves all the love it can get. I think everyone looking for a light hearted and fun drama can give this one a go, I promise it'll melt your heart and the leads' chemistry only adds on to that.

Subbed versions are on Youtube, go ahead and give it a try!! Thank you for supporting them, I'll be waiting for a Season 3 :))))))

" Miss independent Ji Eun season 2 " was pretty entertaining.

The drama didn't follow the first season's story, rather, this one was college-themed. However, they kept the small tips at the end of each episode, which was a unique touch. The main actress was extra cute, especially in those tips scenes, but, overall, she was really the star of the drama. The second couple, though predictable, was sweet too. The performances, finally, were great from all the actors and actresses of the cast.

So, overall, seven out of ten.
