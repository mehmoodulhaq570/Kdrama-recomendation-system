“Cute Bodyguard” tells the love story between Gu Rong, an arrogant and trouble-making rich second generation, and Su Jing Jing, a strong girl who looks gentle and cute. Because of an accident, Su Jing Jing becomes Gu Rong's bodyguard. Though they don't know each other before this accident, romantic love starts as they get involved in each other's life. You Could Hate It Or Like It

This is a drama that you will either hate or like. I would say that the first 11 episode were terrible. I was also ready to give it up. The lead actress is young and the director should have help her with her performance. Her fighting skill should have been better, they look fake. The obsession's with the senior was a turn off. The obsess female vice president was a turn off. they both were made to be strong and independent women but the characters action and charisma turn us off. When I watch the 12 episode and the chemistry between the leads began the drama started to be much better. I really liked the 2nd couple. They were cute how they met and started a relationship. I was happy to see the senior character step away from the leading female character so she could start understanding of the lead male character. From episode 12 on the chemistry between the leads become stronger, and more likeable the couple became. Then the drama became to unravel and all the other characters had much better chemistry with each other. This story is a typical C-drama story were the girl hates the boy at first and then she likes him later. The writer throws in some characters that sometimes weakens the story. I have to say that tis drama is not so bad. After seeing over 700 drama I have seen worse. The male actors Liu Te does keep the story alive, and also many off the experience actors in this drama. They give their character credibility. The female lead is young and by the end her acting is better. The ending was cute.

This was really a cute storyline, I enjoyed it. I needed to smile and this made me smile all the way to the last episode. Liu Te playing Gu Rong Yan is cute and being a new actor, I thought he did very well. LT is very tall at 6.2. As he also towered over Ling Mei Shi who played Su Jing Jing. I loved the strong character she played. The storyline I loved. It was different than other storylines. I love everything Liu Te wore, he looks good in leather. The one outfit that Ling Me Shi wore, the all-black bodyguard outfit, and those boots oh my goodness, those boots were the best thing I have seen in boots in a long time.

Usually, in dramas, I view similar storylines but different actors/actresses with different scenes. This a drama that a family can watch together. A lot of writers are stuck in history with rich versus lowly (poor or middle-class) people. You will love the ending.
