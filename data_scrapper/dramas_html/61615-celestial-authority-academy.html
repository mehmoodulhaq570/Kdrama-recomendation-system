The story is set in the prosperous and peaceful Celestial Dynasty. Celestial Authority Academy was built by the four imperial families, who tried to consolidate their family's position through it. And imperial families fight with one another to get a spot in it. A famous, talented, and well-loved writer (the Empress is one of his hardcore fans!) enrolls in the academy in his own right and without taking the entrance exam. Unexpectedly, this good-looking, gifted young man turns out to be a girl. And many men eventually fall for her. What follows is an insanely sweet, grandiose campus love story. Worth watching at least once!

This drama is quite interesting but compared to other C dramas, the plot isn’t serious enough and feel sth strange (i’m not sure tho what’s weird). If you need a laugh, I recommend trying this. The main lead’s childishness and his friends are hilarious.

What I like most is the OSTs, especially “Qingtan Lake Spring Trip” which was sung by the main actor, Zhou Yanchen/ Johnson. It’s also the opening song of this drama.

I love to see idol producer boys together here and their acting talents are really great. Johnson’s facial expressions and performance really suit his role. But, I feel he deserves a stronger plot next time, if not, his hidden talents are such a pity.

Comparable to "In a Class of Her Own," but not QUITE as good

The plot of this is VERY similar to the c-drama "In a Class of Her Own," where the story's centered around a girl who dresses up as a guy in order to attend an academy. However, differences that I thought made "In a Class of Her Own" better is that "Celestial Authority Academy" is a little more fast-paced, and as a result, I felt that a lot of the plotpoints as well as relationships weren't as developed. There also seemed to be multiple loopholes to the story, and I felt that the ending was a little open-ended (which I guess isn't always a bad thing, I just felt that the last episode went by too fast; we didn't get enough explanation for a lot of things).

However, I did like the cast for this drama, as well as the chemistry between the main couple. All in all, "Celestial Authority Academy" was enjoyable, though I wouldn't say I would rewatch it.
