JYP Entertainment and Mnet’s new boy group survival program is titled “Stray Kids,” which refers to idols who have left home and also captures the mindset of being free from standardization and stereotypes. Rather than individuals surviving to become a team, the trainees will be working towards the goal of debuting all members together. The trainees that will be participating have been revealed to be talented in not only dancing in singing, but producing as well. They will be judged on individual abilities and teamwork based on unexpected missions to win the “trainees vs. JYP” survival. Not only will the program showcase preparation for missions, but it will also give a behind-the-scenes look at the daily lives of trainees. Edit Translation

Watch it at least once

If you're a baby stay or interested in the boys, I think you will enjoy the show.

Objectively, it's a good reality show, nothing new, nothing we haven't seen before in any survival show. But it's the beginning of Stray Kids and the show that introduced us to the boys. If you're excepting drama you will get it, but don't expect it between the members (no fights) (at least not on camera (hyunsung I am looking at you)).

It honestly feels like watching a group of (talented) friends fighting for their dreams. There are laughs, worries, and tears. The performances are also good. Don't expect the same level as now performance-wise because the group was just starting back then. The songs are good though! Also, for me, even if I can recall a lot of little moments that are iconic, I can only think of 3 or 4 performances that are a must-watch for anyone that is not a fan.

So, all in all, as a Stay I hate and love this survival show because I started stanning after watching it in 2017, but they went through a lot :/

But if you want entertainment and want to see how it all started (how far they've come), give it a chance :)
