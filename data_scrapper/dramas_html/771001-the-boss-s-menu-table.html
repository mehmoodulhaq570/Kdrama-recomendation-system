An unpredictable romantic fantasy drama about a 19+ web novel writer, Nam Ja Yeon, who possesses the female protagonist of her own novel, Seo Yeon Seo, and develops a romantic relationship with the male protagonist of her romance novel, Kang Ha Jun. Ja Yeon possesses Seo Yeon Seo but panics when she finds out that the perfect man, Kang Ha Jun in the 19+ web novel, is not her muse, Yeo Ha Jun, but Bok Gyu Hyeon of "No Gain No Love". Kang Ha Jun is the perfect male protagonist in the novel written by Nam Ja Yeon. Kang Ha Jun is the president of Seo Yeon Seo's company, GB Electric, in the novel. He seems cold-hearted but is endlessly affectionate to the woman he loves. He is the perfect chaebol man who meets Yeon Seo and learns of love. He presents exciting chemistry as he faces her sudden change overnight and their single-minded romance. A short yet wild ride of a spinoff

When the spinoff was announced, I was quite eager to watch it. I am glad they didn't make it into a long 16 ep drag of a drama with boring storylines added to it but kept it short , funny and chaotic.
Both leads had good chemistry in the parent story and even though their romance part properly started near the end of the drama it still managed to leave an impression. LSY , PJW and HJH are the perfect trio casting for this one and each did well with their roles.

You don't even need to watch the parent story to watch and enjoy this one.

perfect symbiosis of hot and hilarious

this little spin-off was made for me, and despite wanting to see a full-length one, i'll take it. i said it before, and i'll say it again: the story of ja-yeon and gyu-hyun deserves the best treatment in the world.

spice up our love is a light enhancement to the parent drama, and to be completely honest, even if i didn't watch no gain, no love (thank god i did; such a wholesome romcom), i would've devoured this purely for han ji-hyun and lee sang-yi whose agents should get to work and get them main lead parts in a full-length drama because their joint slay needs to be studied further for obvious scientific purposes.
