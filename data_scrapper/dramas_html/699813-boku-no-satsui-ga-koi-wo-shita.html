Onotora Shu does not know that his foster father is a legendary professional killer. However, his daily life dramatically changes after someone murders this professional killer. Onotora decides to take over the family business and become a hitman in order to avenge his foster father. He is well prepared when he approaches Narumiya Mitsuki who is his assassination target. Despite his physical abilities as a hitman, he is not able to kill her because of his terrible timing, no matter how he tries. On the contrary, he ends up protecting her as danger approaches. The two of them, who should not fall in love, have an unlikely romance. (Source: jdramas.wordpress.com) Edit Translation

Maybe has an awful start but very good in the end.

Okay. I didn't expected I love this drama. Even the rating is one of the lowest in this summer season, but for me this drama not really bad like everyone said. I understand why so many people dropped this drama because in the first three episodes we all know what happen next. For some reasons, he always failed to kill her right? But after the first three episodes , this drama turn out to be unexpected story and getting interesting. The story getting exciting and there's twist too! Some scenes maybe cringe for some of you, but I can understand because there's 'comedy' genre in this drama. The story maybe a bit serious but I like comedy/jokes in this drama as well.

Just look at Shu's silly behaviour enough to make me laughing :') Shu has a naive character, big an empathy, and has a delicate heart make his character really special. Yuko's character also good because she's so firm. Other characters are also quite impressive :D
For chemistry between Taishi & Yuko, everyone said they can't feel their connection and they also lacks of romantic scenes. But for me this is so REAL and MAKE SENSE if we look the situations of these two. They're childhood friends, then they met again but not in a beautiful way. They met again in the situations where the ML must kill her and FL must careful by his kill actions. When we know the behind of story, we still can see they really loves each others. Even they think they have no right to loves him/her because their actions already hurts each others :(
And the last episode wrapped beautifully at least for me. Not too much and not too draggy.

Overall : The rating things can be objective or subjective because it's about everyone taste. Eventho so many dramas have a good ratings because the story itself is actually good. But Boku no Satsui ga Koi wo Shita not really bad like their rating and still worthy to watch! Just give it a chance to this drama, when you guys completed this drama and still not fit with your taste it's okay, I can't force your taste must same as mine, right? :D
So I give my rating 8.5/10 for Boku no Satsui ga Koi wo Shita hehe. Thanks for everyone hardwork for this drama ^^

First time reviewing a show. I'm an avid J-drama and J-film fan but got into hiatus?/MIA. And this is the first drama that I have watched since the last time I watched a J-drama. And boy I got hooked up and maybe get into watching J-dramas again

For starters, this is really a typical J-drama. It has the usual J-drama tropes and Cliches I had seen in most of the j-drama. But in my opinion, what it makes stands above everything else is the characters and the plot.
CHARACTERS
Every character in this show is lovable and rational. I can't really hate the character even the antagonist.
PLOT
I must admit there are a lot of plot holes in this but the main plot or the whole story is really well thought out and really brilliant. I haven't seen this many twists in a J-drama so that's new for me.

And I really like the anime-ish elements in both plot and characters. That element too is well executed in this show.
Bottomline: IT'S REALLY GOOD! definitely recommended
And Vaundy's Hanauranai is so good!
