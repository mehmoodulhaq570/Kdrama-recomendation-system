Two people's lives change their course due to the coming of a comet that takes them into a parallel world where they eventually find true love. Lin Sen He is a man so sharp that he's been hailed as a devil in business since he was a young kid. At the age of 30, he is already a domineering CEO. Bai Xiao Xin is a quirky 20-year-old who's a drama queen. Different from night and day, their paths cross when Bai Xiao Xin lands a gig on Lin Sen He's show. Alas, tragedy strikes, and as Lin Sen He tries to save Bai Xiao Xin from an accident, he is pulled into an ever-changing time and space. (Source: ChineseDrama.info) Edit Translation

I literally made an account to review this lol

I just finished watching this drama, and i was wondering how other people felt about it.
And WOW, i was suprised.
The other reviews for this drama left me in shock.

But I for one, think they didnt give hardly enough credit to this drama.

-Its not exactly the most creative, but all the cliches are done well

-the comedy is spot on, and gives it a nice, light, feel.

-some might argue that the romance takes too long, but i for one think its perfect

-there are a good amount of kiss scenes, and both actor and actress contribute to it

-the kiss scenes arent super...violent? Idk but there are so many kiss scenes in dramas where the couple looks likes they're having a seizure or something

-No evil mother/father in law

-the villains add an element of mystery that makes it really intersting, and helps keep the story going

-the chemistry between the couple is amazing

-the way they fall in love is actually pretty realistic and sweet

-the female lead isnt super dumb
(She can actually be quite crafty at times)

-Both leads are very likeable

-there is technically a second male lead and a female one, but they dont really FEEL like they are. If anything, theyre just side characters.

Anyway, i hope this helped!
I highly recommend it!

deja vu like 3 times ?

This drama wasn't a bad watch but the story line frustrates me!!!
1) its only 20 episodes but their romantic development doesnt start till like 12 episodes in ? ugggh?? lol it all the sweet stuff in the last like 5 episode
2) development of the story had loopholes lol I mean because this story is about them falling into a loophole/comet which transfers them back to the moment they met . Basically the female lead is a youtuber/vlogger/does live stream for her job and she interviews under the main lead's company. They start off on a bad foot, and due to the male lead saving the female lead from drowning they enter a loophole/comet of some sort that brings them back to the moment of the interview.... Thus the male lead wants nothing to deal with female lead/ tries to avoid the female lead as much as possible. of course you know he cant really hide it , slowly but surely they develop feelings for each other... All at the same time male lead company, someone is up to no good trying to bring him down/ cause danger to him. It turns out they are also after the female lead for having a very important usb that the evil dude wants. So the evil dude sends people to go after the leads. Eventually they fall into danger and ends up in deja vu / comet so they are once again back to the same first scene. This time again only the male lead remembers, so he actively pursues the female who is slightly hesitant..
Romantic scenes are super cute and sweet. I really admired Zhang yu Jian 's kissing scenes because its believable and passionate and not stiff. lol i guess he has softer/flexible lips >_< dont call me a perv lol. but i find a lot of male leads kissing to be very stiff or just a peck with no emotions. For the female lead she is younger so i dont really feel the connection as much...

Zhang Yu jian ( male lead ) acting is what kept me through this drama. He is super cute when he overly reacts. Like reacting to running into the female lead. Lol his hair is parted really cute and dorky in this drama. The female lead at times seems kinda kiddish and annoying , she's more whiny sounding.

Overall, this drama would of been better if they used only 2 times having deja vu/comet/loop hole. and the romantic development is way to slow... but still it was a ok watch .... just a tad disappointed...

i forgot to add , I COULDNT STAND HER NAILS IN THIS DRAMA. OMG SO CREEPY. ITS LONG AND WHITE. ONE SCENE YOU COULD ALMOST SEE RIGHT THROUGH THEM LOL
