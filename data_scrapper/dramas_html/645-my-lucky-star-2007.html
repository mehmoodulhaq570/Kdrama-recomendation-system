Like the boy who cried wolf, Xia Zhi Xing had lied one too many times. So when she did tell the truth, nobody believed her except for the most unlikely person, Zhong Tian Qi, a runaway young master of a jewelry empire. His belief in her inspired her to become a real jewelry designer instead of a lying, scheming jewelry con artist. In the process, she taught him the real value of jewels that he is destined to inherit. Edit Translation

I love the music (downloaded on my phones), I love the acting (especially the facial expressions) so hilarious and i love the actors too. The story is uniquely cliche. In general: boy meets girl, poor vs. rich, good vs. evil, love triangle, and damsel in distress are factors that making this underrated! guess what!

DO NOT BE FOOLED!!!!

It is unique in its cliche way! and way way to go!
I am trying to convince you guys! just watch the first episode and you will see!Am i being biased? of course I am! why wouldn't I!

This is the only drama that I will readily rewatch and the story although an "old concept" was really fresh. The acting was amazing by all parties -villains and good guys alike. There weren't really any moments in this drama that were too unrealistic, although a few scenes do teter close to the edge dividing what's realistic and unrealistic. This was even better in my book than Autumn's Concerto, even though the plot lines are somewhat different.

The only real complaint I have with this drama is the music got a bit repetive, but otherwise I loved this drama through and through. This is a must watch drama and I highly recommend it.
