This is may be a cliche for some and maybe new plot for another, let me be honest, i am an addict for soaps, and since i am an experienced watcher, i have seen such drama before, but what really makes it enjoyable, re-watchable is actually the whole as a package.
Story: Although the story is a cliche for Uee, (her specialty poor and miserable then a happy ending) she always keep improving every time. The plot is well executed and well written.
Characters: All have developed, all characters had closures. except for two characters stood the same.
Plot twist: There are few plot twists along the way. it made the series freshen up when it dragged a bit.
Re-watch value: it is totally re-watchable, its one of the series that you will not bored of.
The team work of the cast is super effective for the near perfection of the series.
The music though, i thought it would be more remarkable OST, i only had one OST by heart, not all of them.
All in All, this is a very likable experience and i definitely recommended

What a serious disappointment. This started off great, but after 20 episodes or so began to use the same tropes over and over again....like MULTIPLE times. Needless to say, it was super repetitive. There was a grandmother with dementia whose dementia episodes were hilarious, but got old real fast. The female lead was an absolute doormat. And don't get me started on the antagonists. These women were shameless and hypocritical. They showed no remorse whatsoever. I don't even think they learned their lesson. I kept waiting for karma to hit them like a bus, but it did not happen sadly...or at least not to the extent that I wanted. The secondary couple gave me life though. I loved them way better than the main couple and they are probably the main reason I kept watching. I have a higher standard for weekend family dramas, but My Only One seems like it was a daily soap that was slightly altered to fit the weekend drama time slot. With the way it was written, it is more suited as a daytime daily drama.
