Love comes in all flavors. Secret Ingredient is the story of a Korean man who gives up his entire luxurious life in Seoul in search of his childhood girlfriend, someone he’s never forgotten all these years, in the hopes that she would fall in love with him, not for who they were back then or who they are now, but for what they can aspire to be: happy, fulfilled and blissfully in love with one another. Food is a Secret Expression of Love & Triumph

Just as how this short but very meaningful series have shown its viewers. Food can indeed be an ecpression of love. just how one can be comforted fron hunger, pain and weariness. The series was able to relate how food have comforted Ha Joon of his cravings when he was young. It also showed how cooking and giving food to a weary heart like how Ha Joon's mom was somewhat relieved from tasting the food prepared by her son.
