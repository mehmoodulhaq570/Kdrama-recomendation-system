Pimlapas, a once successful hi-so girl, loses everything in a single day: her father dies, leaving her penniless due to her stepmother's schemes, and her boyfriend dumps her. Desperate, she accepts a mysterious offer to steal documents from Ramet, a wealthy hotel owner. Pimlapas seduces him for the job but unexpectedly falls in love. When she attempts the theft, Ramet catches her but, instead of calling the police, makes her work at his hotel to repay her debt. Just as things improve, her stepmother reappears, bringing chaos back into her life. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation
