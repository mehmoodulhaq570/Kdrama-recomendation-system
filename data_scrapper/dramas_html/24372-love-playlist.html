A short web drama, though it has now season two and three as well. The story is close to the school stories of the other playlist dramas, however it takes a more mature approach. The love stories, the friendships, the love triangles and the relationship hardships were all entertaining, heartwarming and sometimes cute. The couples were nice too, and some of them had good chemistry. The final episode kind of left things unconcluded, so you have (and you probably want to) watch the other seasons as well. Also, the concept with the playlists was really clever and cute.
