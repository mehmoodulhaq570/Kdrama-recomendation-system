Watch this fun bad show

This show was a roller coaster. The way the story took a 180 turn was crazy. And I didn’t hate it, because it was actually really interesting but it was just so unexpected. The one thing I did hate was the fact that the actors could not act AT ALL. Literally everyone, except one person, looked so awkward in their acting. I feel bad for the writers because the shows storyline was good, it’s just the actors made these scenes look so bad that it was actually funny. Not personally hating on the actors though, just saying to do better next time. Anyways, I would say watch it because it was fun to watch

You can try it....

This drama is not as bad as it seemed to me after reading comments...... And i am glad i watched it ignoring the comments...
ofc the last 2,3 episodes were unexpected but they were not bad....and it was not dark tbh..
There had to be some reason for male to be with female afterall....
And even though he came for revenge... But he didn't.... He always protected female and didn't let anything bad happen to her....
And he didn't came to revenge his ex lover or crush, but for a girl who was like a sister to him and who helped him when he had nobody....
Female is the only girl he loved...
And at the end.... They both reconciled.....

In my opinion... This drama is good....
Neither too sweet not too bitter...

Kisses could have been better....
And romance too but it was not bad either...

Overall... It was a fine drama....
Neither tooo bad nor too great...
