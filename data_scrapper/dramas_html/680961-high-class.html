Song Yeo Ul’s life unravels when she is falsely accused of murdering her husband. Nam Ji Sun is the influential “queen bee” among the mothers at the international school attended by Song Yeo Ul’s son. Danny O, a teacher at the school and a former ice hockey player, is also part of the story. Hwang Na Yun, a single mother, is the only one who shows kindness to Song Yeo Ul. Cha Do Yeong, a former top actress, clings to Nam Ji Sun in hopes of reclaiming her place in the limelight. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

Too predictable...ugh

Disappointed to say the least, I really wanted to like this show but i just could not bring myself to.
I had great expections when i heard about the drama. The cast and the "plot" seemed promising but as I watched the first couple of episodes and I felt so let down.

I expected a lot more but got offered the same story and plot devices as the other shows in the genre.
I was intrigued but felt bored at the same time. It is decent to watch but not enough to keep me wanting more nor anticipating the next episode.

The plot became too predictable and the characters became annoying. Cough the detective cough cough. Unnecessary actions and scenes like it is literally just a school and they are all fighting like this. The female is so timid like why? The some of the characters lack sense, like they so dumb. Just poorly written

It started off well but sccumbed in the end.

This actually a thriller that is very well executed.
Revolves around a bunch of high class people who have all the money in this world to send their kids to an over the top international school which accepts children only by invites! The MFL's husband's death has brought in a lot of troubles for her. but she decides to fight all the odds for her only son. later in the episodes till ep 6,a lot of mystery unfolds.

Honestly, the plot was gripping. acting is good. the cinematography is excellent. With every passing episodes you'll realise that the FL is a strong person but
It becomes a flop in later episodes to an extent where you will skip scenes and yawn!Came back to elaborate my thoughts after ep 6, one episode is a total filler, and this goes a straight down hill after ep 9. There is no thrill left and it becomes a drama where the protagonist wants to get the criminal behind bars.

I had a lot of hopes for this, somehow i am not going to remember even watching it. This is clearly not written well towards the end.
