All of the ARMY out there, this is the best show of the group that you must watch if you haven't already!
Being a hardcore fan of BTS, this show has always been on my "to watch" list. I regret not seeing it sooner. This is the season 1 where the guys go to explore Northern Europe. As always with the boys, the show is very entertaining, doesn't lack humor (believe me, a goldmine for memes), and surprisingly enough, it's very inspirational. I want to travel to the same countries too. Watching back, you do reflect on some things and fall in love with the band members even more. <3

BTS Fun

This was a fun show to watch. New and old fans alike will enjoy. This is the guys first trip where they had to do do things on their own (all the while being filmed). I laughed and had teary eye moments especially when RM couldn't stay on the last few day days of the trip. LMAO when J-hope's way to avoid pictures with fans and randos is to say "important business"! Loved when V got lost and instead of panicking he just enjoyed the place he was in. I stan V and one of the things I love about him is his ability to just go with the flow. I love how Jimin lost his bag and almost lost it again. Being responsible for your traveling gear and documents is huge! I loved when they went to the sauna in Finland and made the bet to jump in the cold water and Suga was like I hate cold water and of course had to get in.

I loved that it was a vacation where they got to have fun and all the reflection they had where they expressed the joy and appreciation for being able to go and all the people who usually take care of them when they travel.

I loved all the dancing and being silly and just being together. All the pranks they pulled and tried to pull on each other. Good show!
