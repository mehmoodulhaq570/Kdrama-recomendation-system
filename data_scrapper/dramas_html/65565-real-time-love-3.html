What to expect.

1) This drama gives second male lead vibes super badly. You never know what is right or wrong and for a moment, you start to make up a story to fill in these blank spaces in the book. It just tells us what things may be and some things may not be what people show it to be.

2) Let’s forget about everything and just appreciate how many audiences would rather choose second male leads than first male leads because the first belongs to the female lead and the second belongs to everyone.

3) We can expect a lot of other ships and other relationship puzzles. We have so much relationship problems that my brain is more puzzled than the puzzle itself.

LETS ALL ROOT FOR OUR BOY YOOK DEOKJIN! He got those eyes that second male lead syndrome patients go after. We just go for the second male lead, it doesn’t matter who is the male lead we go for the second male lead.

Bad ending..

Bad ending to be honest. Season 3 started at a weird point but got better as it went on, but it declined pretty quickly, I really didn't like what Yeoni did at the end. I dislike Hongyun way less now but she's still a bit annoying, her character is growing on me and I'm starting to see her personality. I think I'll dislike Yechan if his choice is different though. Started extremely good i liked it a lot the acting was very good but fell flat quickly, I think it wrapped up too quick. I honestly think Hongyun and Deokjin ended up having better chemistry than Hongyun and Yechan, I'm interested to see how season 4 is. Overall it was quite good, a bit better than season 2, I recommend.
