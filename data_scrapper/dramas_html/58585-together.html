With You tells the stories of the Chinese military and civilians in the fight against the COVID-19 through narrative atoms. During the fight against the epidemic, there were medical workers, medical teams, military doctors, common takeaway deliverymen, vehicle volunteers, ordinary citizens, construction workers, disease control, community and public security personnel, young volunteers and workers of mask production. These ordinary people constitute the main force to fight the pandemic in all walks of life across the country, and have made indelible contributions to the fight against the pandemic. (Source: WeTV) ~~ Based on real people and stories during the fight against the new coronavirus epidemic in mainland China. Edit Translation

Thoughts

This is probably one of my favorite real-life problem dramas that lets you experience what is happening during Covid-19. After watching episodes of this drama, I actually felt the difficulties of the people there. It's something I wouldn't ever want to experience... the pain... the sorrow.... every bit left me in tears. Sending your own family off into the hospitals... worrying if they're make it alive or not... that's something truly... truly miserable...
Seeing a family reunion during that time makes the tears flow quicker.... during January of 2019, the volunteers, nurses, doctors, and people from parts of China were unable to visit their families of distant lands. Who would ever want to experience that? All the actors brought this experience to me..
I would highly recommend you watch this if you're interested in Chinese society during the epidemic.
I really want to say that I would like to appreciate the healing angels, the volunteers, and the helpers. Without them, what would life be today? Imagine the epidemic today.... Although the virus is maintained in China, it's still occurring where I live. I just hope this day would be over.
Who knew this drama came out when the virus isn't contained where I live.
At last, I would like to say thank you for the directors of this drama. Thank you for bringing such an experience to me. I was so naive about what was going on during that time. Who knew 2021 turned out to be like this? Thank you for letting me experience a person's life through a screen. I'll be continuing watching this drama. Although I wouldn't necessarily rewatch this drama.... I wouldn't want to experience more pain and sadness.
