The son of a retail tycoon and an e-commerce live-streamer join hands on a road to realizing their entrepreneurial dreams. Qian Xi Xi comes from a broken family. At a young age, she was sent to live in Duan Ran's home and they grew up together. However, their personalities are like heaven and earth due to their difference in status. Through sheer coincidence, Qian Xi Xi gets into the field of live-streaming. Duan Ran who's a fuerdai (second-generation rich) sees the potential in e-commerce live streaming and acquires the company, thus becoming Qian Xi Xi's boss. Duan Ran realizes that the industry is more complicated than he ever imagined. Meanwhile, Qian Xi Xi throws herself into her work and unexpectedly encounters top star Jiang Zhe Yang who was her old high school classmate. Duan Ran and Qian Xi Xi encounter repeated problems at work and also in their relationship that they must learn to overcome. Same old, with some fun and cuteness

This drama is definitely nothing extraordinary, but it does make you cheer on the main leads.

The leads are very cute and funny, and you actually see them grow whenever they face difficulties and it's nice, when you see people learn from their mistakes.

Xixi, the lead gal, starts very cute and very idealistic. She is naive and knows nothing about business (but do remember that while she has some practical experience, she has none in management or any former backgroundin business) which leads to mistakes and many insecurities, and that makes her relatable. I caught myself thinking, what would I do in her shoes, with her knowledge and monetary assets? And that made me be more sympathetic with her character.

Duan Ran, was so arrogant in the beginning, that I actually did not like him or his treatment of the FL. I think he contributed to a lot of her insecurities, however he does change and becomes one of her big supporters, and that is cute and lovable. He is a very lovable guy and you can't help liking him in the end.

The rest of the cast was great, I was engaged in their side stories and lives, and that is another positive point, because it kind of complements the main story.

The villains and their tricks are quite realistic and definitely something that could happen so I was happy with how grounded they were with the plot.

It's a cute, business drama, that is worth of your time, even if you have to fast-foward some parts!
