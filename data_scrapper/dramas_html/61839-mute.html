Han Na is a female concert organizer who has an incredible secret ability: when she encounters a person whom she loves, she can “hear” their thoughts. While trying to snare a prestigious full-time job, she has a chance encounter with a man named Je Won, who has trouble expressing his feelings and suffers from a hearing-related problem. When they meet, Han Na discovers that she can read his mind, and when they are together, Je Won’s hearing problems magically disappear. A romantic bond slowly develops between them. However, after they kiss for the first time, Han Na discovers that she can no longer read Je Won’s mind. Will love continue to bloom for Han Na even after she stops “hearing” his innermost thoughts? A Nice Easy Watch

Mute is a short drama that will take about an hour of your time. I found the drama charming and I liked the leads. Though the limited time limits the development of the leads, I felt it was enough to properly tell the story. The story itself is both original and derivative. What I mean by that, the premise itself is original but how it was executed will seemed similar to other Kdramas. If you are looking for a short drama to enjoy, I recommend this one. I may even rewatch this one in the future.

A quick, sweet show to watch

Do you need a break between angst-ridden shows? This might just be the perfect show for you to watch. This was a beautifully done short show. It really gave me all the feels and I found myself smiling through the whole thing. Since it was a short show, it was nice to not have to slog through the cliches and filler you find in a lot of the longer shows. She was just darling, big beautiful eyes and a very expressive face. He was so sweet, and that honey-dipped voice, wow! They were so cute together and this show is totally worth your time!
