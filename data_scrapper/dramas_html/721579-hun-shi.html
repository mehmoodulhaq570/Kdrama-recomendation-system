Short and binged worthy suspenseful drama to watch, especially the acting skill of ML.

Dear Liar is a short web suspenseful drama about a husband who lies, is manipulative and leaves many broken hearts until he meets his wife, Song Yi Fei.

I first watched Fan Zhi Xin in "My Lethal Man" and thought he did an awesome job and had great potential to be a good actor. He did not disappoint me in Dear Liar.

The actress Qu Meng Ru who played his wife, Song Yi Fei, did a good job, and the supporting cast is also decent.

Each episode is about 10 minutes, and I binged watch the entire drama (27 episodes) in one sitting. I enjoyed it and look forward to Fan Zhi Xin's future dramas.

I am giving it a 7.5 rating.
