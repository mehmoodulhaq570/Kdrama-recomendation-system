Hu Tian Tian foresaw the end of the Hu family, with the whole family being slaughtered in the chess game for power. In order to change fate, Tian Tian wins over Su Yan Li, a fallen prince who has also experienced "rebirth" and strives to guide the villain to do good. However, Su Yan Li might be the culprit behind the demise of the Hu family. As the two were in an intense tug-of-war, Tian Tian gradually learned about her and Su Yan Li’s fate. Great Premonition, costume production

This is a great but somewhat peculiar Premonition, costume production with good actors and a storyline that differs from most.
I admit that I found it to be somewhat confusing at first because the “premonitions”/dreams came without a warning, and changed from the original one.
I liked this kind/protective FL and strong/intense ML. The chemistry was good.
I enjoyed this one.

Could have been better

I nearly made it to the end, but lost interest halfway through. The storyline is actually quite intiguing, if you are an enemies-to-lovers fan like me. However, the execution failed.

If you are familiar with those short cdramas, you already are prepared not to expect too much. Nonetheless, I ended up being disappointed.

FL seemed to naive and clumsy to me. ML could not be taken seriously in scenes in which he was supposed to be dangerous. Her oldest brother could also not be taken seriously - iykyk. The other characters had either been unnesessary or frankly annoying. The whole acting seemed off to me.

Had more potential.
