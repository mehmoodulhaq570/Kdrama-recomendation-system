Ah Jung is an un-employed young woman who is looking for a roommate. Her tendency to be over affectionate with boyfriends irritates them and the latest boyfriend also dumps her at a coffee shop. Coffee shop manager co-worker Geum Dol has a special ability to shapeshift into a dog and he offers comfort to heartbroken Ah Jung as a dog. What will Ah Jung do when she finds the truth? This started off as a fun concept but it was quickly bogged down by the way it kept skipping to the future at the start of each episode and then going back, like rewinding a tape. There was also way too much filler, and the same scenes over and over, stupid flashbacks and repeats we don't need to see. The episode with the second male lead was a nice break cause at least we explored something new with him. Also the leads were kinda meh, no chemistry, I suppose you could say it was puppy love in the fact that it was tame and unexciting.

The story has a cute premise, and it really is interesting. Having a different than usual concept, it is quite interesting. As it is just a web drama, it really is so short for the story to fully develop. I think the romance is the only thing that they want the viewers to see. I am not a fan of the acting, I think it is because they are all rookies. The male lead is fine, but the female lead is so awkward. The other couples also shown in the drama did not really caught my attention. Not a fan of the drama.
