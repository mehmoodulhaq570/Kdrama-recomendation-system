Deeply in love, Park Ga Ram and Kim Seon Jae anticipated their wedding, but a tragic accident shattered their dreams. Ga Ram didn't survive, and Seon Jae was left in a coma, unaware he was fighting for his life alone. Ga Ram finds herself in an unfamiliar place, approached by a stranger named X with an offer to save Seon Jae. To do so, she must relive her last week on Earth and break up with him. Returning to Seon Jae, she faces the heartbreaking challenge of parting with her beloved before it's too late. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

Welcome to my rant about how this is a hidden jewel... So let's start:
I have never loved a villain more than X. I wish we knew more about his story, why was he like that, who is the girl who hurt him, etc? Surely he was supposed to be bad but I can't help to make excuses for him. He was so intriguing and especially on the last episode I got goosebumps when he yelled at Ga Ram.
The acting was amazing given it was a miniseries and sometimes the acting is not a strong point. You could feel the emotions from the actors and I was sooooo ready to shread some tears but held back. The raw emotions of love was overwhelming but at the same time I couldn't understand how their life didn't matter if the other one lived. I have been in love but never at this scale. How can someone be happy knowing that another person sacrificed their life for them to live, that's a huge burden to bear.
Definitely will binge watch it again ☺️

Boring, and it was only 10 episodes!

The first couple of episodes were them fighting. I wasn't feeling that they were a good couple to begin with, so when she was trying to make a deal to save him, it just didn't make any sense to me. Why were they together? I didn't believe that they were in love, due to the circular storyline (break up, make up, repeat), the lack of chemistry and the fact that they were "broken up" more than they were together. I felt that the writers were trying to create a thought-provoking drama, and granted, the potential was there, but if you can't see the love between the couple, the rest of it just doesn't make sense.
