Shen Xi is a peppy college student majoring in dance and loves to eat just a little too much. She has a boyfriend named Lin Yu Tang, who is very caring and sweet. He Zhi Zhou is an aloof, moody engineering major at a nearby university. One day Shen Xi and He Zhi Zhou suddenly swapped souls in front of a mysterious mirror. Zhi Zhou finds himself in the body of Shen Xi, and Shen Xi finds herself inside the body of Zhi Zhou. What will become of this soul-swapping duo? And what will happen with the relationship between Shen Xi and Lin Yu Tang? Edit Translation

I thought this was a webtoon adaptation, but I was surprised to find out it's not! It has like an "anime/webtoon/manga" style (I don't know how to describe it), that I find is often very hard to pull off but this show does it effortessly. The sound effects, the timing, the editing and the acting are hilarious, the cinematography is lovely and I really like that the comedy stays until the end, and it gets better and better with each episode. The show doesn't take itself too seriously, and I feel like it's lowkey a parody ("Where did you get this car from? Don't ask so much, the plot needs it!") The style of this drama really stands out, so sad that is so underrated! To me is a hidden gem, i'm so glad I watched it

Highly recommended!
