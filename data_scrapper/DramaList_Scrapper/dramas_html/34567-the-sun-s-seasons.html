Kim Yoo Wol became an orphan and he was raised by foster parents. He worked extremely hard and passed his CPA exam. He enjoys happy days with his girlfriend Yoon Shi Wol, but he has a near-death experience. He changes his life and goes under the alias of Oh Tae Yang. He is dead to Yoon Shi Wol. Meanwhile, Yoon Shi Wol, who believes her boyfriend Kim Yoo Wol is dead, has a difficult time. She begins to work at the conglomerate company Yangji Group. There, she meets Oh Tae Yang. I am currently watching it so, I don’t know what kind of ending to come, but i don't think i will be disappointed.

A Place In The Sun is a beautiful story of love, romance, deception, envy, jealousy, secrets, pride, arrogance, greed, anger, frustration, joy and strife that took place in a conglomerate family in Korea. The script is well written and the drama scenes have been acted well. The plot is interesting and intriguing and each episode is engrossing. This is a story worthy of watching and for me, this drama has succeeded in invoking my feelings and emotions - love, fear, anger, sympathy, joy and satisfaction.
