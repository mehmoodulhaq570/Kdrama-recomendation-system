fun watch!

To be honest, I wasn't expecting much from this web series, but I was pleasantly surprised. While the plot wasn't very well developed in my opinion, all the main characters had distinct personalities that were well integrated into each short story. The drama itself was short and simple. It didn't need an elaborate plot to be enjoyable. Each episode was entertaining and made me laugh. I have also never watched a series with such short episodes, but it was nice as a little pick me up at the end of the day. I would definitely recommend this if you are looking for a little laugh! Hoping to see more from these actors in the future!

a pleasant surprise

When starting this drama I had no expectations, giving it 20 episodes of 5 minutes I thought that it will be a mediocre show I watch in the meantime. What really shocked me is how great the story was compared to being a comedy. The writers did a great job, I never got bored and always looked forward to the next episode! The last episode was heartwarming and ended the drama well, so I have no regrets whatsoever.

This show is not a melancolic, long one, so it is a great call for fans of comedies, or anyone who just wants to relax a little between two dramas. I enjoyed the concept, as well as the characters.!
