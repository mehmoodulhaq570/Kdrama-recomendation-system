They simply know how to cater to their audience better than any other ship out there. Gun is shameless and Papii knows when to reel him in. A simple premise: let's have Off and Gun interview people in bed. Genius! Hilarious and endearing. Off and Gun know how to loosen the reins within a friendship and they make every episode a pleasure to watch with cute adlibs and funny rivalries. Rewatch value is a 100/10 cause I definitely cannot stop watching it. The episode with Oab is particularly telling.
