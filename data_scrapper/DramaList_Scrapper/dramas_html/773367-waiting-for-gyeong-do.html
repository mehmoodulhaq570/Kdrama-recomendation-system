Lee Gyeong Do and Seo Ji U, who dated twice when they were twenty and twenty-eight and had broken up, end up reuniting as a reporter who reported an adultery scandal and the wife of the scandal's protagonist, and their heartbreaking and touching romance. Lee Gyeong Do is an entertainment reporter for Dongwoon Ilbo. Gyeong Do is someone with a personality that is down-to-earth and of personable nature. His life takes a chaotic turn when he encounters his ex-girlfriend Seo Ji U, with whom he once shared a passionate and turbulent romance, due to a scandal involving Ji U, his first love, and her husband.
