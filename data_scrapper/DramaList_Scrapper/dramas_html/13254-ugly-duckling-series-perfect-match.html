After undergoing plastic surgery that left her face severely damaged due to an allergic reaction, Junita, nicknamed Junior, faces social rejection and ridicule. To escape the scrutiny of Bangkok, she convinces her mother to let her attend a rural college and seek treatment from a doctor recommended by the Ugly Duckling forum. There, she meets Suea, a charming and broke senior who accepts her for who she is. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

I went into this drama with no expectations. I just thought the title was cute. What I discovered was too much cuteness. Mook was a great heroine. She was sweet, but complex enough you could really identify with her. Push was everything. He was charming, funny, sweet and a total pain in the ass! Their chemistry was undeniable. The best support was Bee. He's the friend I wish I had. Seriously people watch this. It's short and sweet, but complicated enough to keep you engaged. Plus you'll fall in love with the cast!

This was a completely fun Thai drama I've become a avid fan of Puttichai Put's growing body of work and his performance in this will not let you down. I'm looking forward to watching the rest of the dramas in this four part series. Fun storyline and fun casting are poured into this. I was able to find it translated on youtube I hope at some point Viki will add this into their line up for I really hope they expand soon on their collection of dramas / lakorns from Thailand.

The music in this pretty much had like it's main theme song that carried through it. But as far as rewatch value goes it's one of those fun and short dramas you could watch again and again for it didn't have annoying crazy girls or overly evil mother or any of that it was just a fun watch on young relationships and friendships I'd give it a 10 on it being a current view on Thailand dating.
