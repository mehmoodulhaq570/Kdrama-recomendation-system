I loved it!!!

The mini drama is really good! A story about fate, love and promises.

When I planned on watching this I was thinking this would be a story about a father and a daughter or a boy and his little sister since the girl in the cover seemed to be a lot younger than Jinyoung, but even being a love story it's still amazing!

To be honest, the reason why I watched this was because Jinyoung was one of the actors, but I loved the drama! And the other actors are also very good at acting, the girls who played the role of Eundong were all so pretty!!!

The story itself was beautiful, but the actors, the music, the scenarios and everything else made it even prettier! It was so interesting that I was paying attention the whole time. And since it was easy to watch I could do other things at the same time without getting lost in the story.

I liked the music, it was cute, soft and it perfectly fitted the story.

The last episode made me want to watch the actual drama so bad!

This is extraordinary because it shows that there's no shame about being poor and that we should care and protect the ones we love because one day, without realizing, they could disappear.

It was very inspiring for me because the fact that he never gave up on finding Eundong and he did everything he could to find her.

I recommend this because it's short but still sweet and introduces us to the actual drama which I plan on watching soon!

I would rewatch it because it's short yet cute!
