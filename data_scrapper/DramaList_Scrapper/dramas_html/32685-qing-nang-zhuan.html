Mu Xing Chen, a healer from the sacred Shen Mu Tribe, blends Qing Nang Shu with Eastern and Western medicine to cure the sick. After rescuing Ye Yun Shang, a young doctor’s daughter, he flees his politically charged family to live freely. Their journey leads them to a plague-stricken region, where Xing Chen faces numerous challenges in his quest to master medicine. Along the way, he finds love while confronting personal and professional trials. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

This was an amazing piece. Although some parts of it were unbearable, I still loved it. Both main leads had great acting. To be completely honest, the reason for this high review is because of the surprises there were on the way. If I were to rewatch it, I predict that that would be years later when I've forgotten all context. I would recommend this to anyone who likes a wuxia kind of drama. There weren't a lot of kiss scenes for those wondering. I feel that the story plot was a great idea. It was mostly about the clan/tribe in the forest that had this magical fruit. I feel that it also represents the actual society well. The waste we emit is impacting the world a lot and this story shows it.

Story: The story was interesting and it had quite a few plot twists. The scriptwriter really worked and built the relationship of the main leads so it's convincing and cute. I found myself relating to a lot of characters throughout the story. I would say the ending could've been better but I've seen worse. (SPOILER: it's a happy ending) The first few episodes were amazing. I couldn't stop watching but once I hit around episode 10, it started to feel dragged. Luckily the entire story is only 36 episodes or else I probably would've dropped it. If you power through it, you'll be satisfied. I feel that the story was very unique and cute. A good show to watch with a few skips.

Acting/Cast: I would say that the acting was very good. The main leads were perfect. The others were convincing. It was like a roller coaster with these actors and actresses. The evil ones were pretty good at hiding it.

Music: The music was very fitting and I loved it.

Overall, I recommend this to people who like magical dramas!! It's shorter than most chinese dramas and that's what makes it so great.
