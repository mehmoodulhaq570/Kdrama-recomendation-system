Give it a try

Honestly seeing the reviews made me postpone watching this drama for awhile, so for anyone who is hesitanting pls give it a chance. Rating in this site really don't corrisponde to the quality of the dramas!
ML has a double role one as quilong ( present) and the other zhang (past) and I personally think he nailed the execution of both I give him a b+ just because sometimes he would go over with zhang, like he would be silent and way too collected but still I got immersed in it and could differentiate the two of them
FL(1&2) we have 2 Female leads here one is wanglin (present) and one from the past (ziyi) played by different actresses. I have no problem with ziyi acting i think she did well BUT God was her whispering voice annoying at the beginning and some parts but I got used to it after a while, as for wanglin I love her character more at the early episode i think the actress couldn't really act some scenes so I skipped some stuff tho not much.
Now for the ending I don't want to give spoiler but big reason why I give this an 8.5 instead of 9 is the ending with ziyi which I will not reveal but wish they just rolled with what happened already without the restart thing
Overall it's a very light hearted watch especially if you like time travel like me, I loved it and I think I will re-watch it soon
