Handsome Senkawa Kanji is a dangerous intimidator who will solve cases which the police and investigators are unable to by finding people’s weaknesses and manipulating them with threatening words. Kanesaka Mio, a good, nice girl who grew up in a wealthy family, attends the department of education at a university. She works part-time and lives modestly but can move money when needed. She not only refuses to overlook the slightest bad thing but will also not leave anyone who is in difficulty alone. This results in her always butting into the predicaments of complete strangers and getting involved in cases. Senkawa and Mio are completely incompatible and should not meet but they get caught in all sorts of cases from bank transfer scams to kidnappings. ~~ Adapted from the light novel "Ima kara Anata wo Kyouhaku Shimasu" by Namiya Fujiishi. One Word: Hilarious

This drama was absolutely funny. I really loved the relationship between the main guy and girl. A righteous rich girl and a con artist, seems like an oddly pair. His whole team was weird in a good way. They used illegal means to help those the law left behind but came upon some goofy circumstances. I really liked watching this drama. I wish we had a bit more depth to the characters but overall still a really good drama.

I would definitely recommend watching if you want a drama to watch that's not heavy or melodramatic. I watched this show on a whim and doesn't regret it at all.

BIG CITY HUNTER VIBES.

What I noticed first and foremost while watching this drama was the strong likeliness to Tsukasa Hojo's City Hunter.

The main character is a baddie with a soft heart working as an outlaw, weak with girls, children and old people, who ends up helping the FL out of a pinch. Just like Saeba Ryo.

We also get a nice and intriguing back story for the FL (just like Makimura Kaori).

Add to the mix some action /fighting scenes, humor and puns, banter, a slight romance, plus a support cast, and you get the perfect mix!

Some might find the FL annoying because of her values, but remember that imperfect characters are the best, because that leave place for them to grow.

As for the core mechanism used by the hero (threatening people, yes, that's the spoiler you're looking for, but wasn't sure it was one since it's mentioned in the title tbh) it was quite surprising and unusual, but nonetheless really fun!

A really fun ride from beginning to the end, and if you loved it, go watch Tantei ga Hayasugiru. It's also really fun in it's own way!
