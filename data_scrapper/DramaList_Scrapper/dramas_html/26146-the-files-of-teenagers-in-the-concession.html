In early Republican-era Shanghai, a city fractured by foreign-run concessions and rampant crime, Wu Qian enters a shady competition to save his best friend's ailing grandmother. His involvement pulls him into a deadly clash between foreign powers and local police. Along the way, he meets He Hong Yi, a passionate freedom fighter, and joins her cause to resist the oppressive system. As tensions rise, the two are drawn into a perilous battle for the city's future. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

Pretty Good

This show was really good the acting was good. All good stuff, I would recommend it. My only problem is that it dragged on for far too long. Towards the end, you think oh ok it's getting close but they kept adding new characters. The last villain honestly could've wrapped up way faster but no, they prolonged it. I'm also not even sure how it ended, there's a lot of plot holes. Killed way too many characters to be allowed. Overall pretty good I would watch. Still kinda mad that I watched 58 episodes just for it to basically have an open ending
