The backdrop is highschool and Cha Ga Eun is the protagonist who is pretty much an introvert. The story shows us that we must treasure the ones beside us and open up to them. When Ahn Do Young was found by the notorious Sae Ho at first he thought of revealing that Do Young was a robot but then he chooses not to. The key here is confidentiality and friendship. There's a lot of hilarious situations with Do Young's cuteness.
The actors and their interactions were cute, we can watch them on and on. I'm seriously rooting for a season 2!!!

I watched these series after watching SWEET REVENGE 2 and they were really cute. I really liked it. The story of the drama was really cute and it was a good one too. The acting of the actors was good too. JI MIN HYUK was looking really good in the drama and he really pulled off the character of the robot pretty well. The lead actress was really beautiful and her acting was cute too. The rest of the cast was good too. But I was a little disappointed with the ending of the drama. The ending starting to sadden me as he ( DO YOUNG ) wasn't allowed to meet GA EUN but he comes back in the last. So, it was a happy ending after all but I was confused that how was he able to come as he had two options : to stay with her with erased memory or away from her with her memories that's why. But overall, it was a good drama so I recommend it.
