Nat Sirsukmaha-anand M.D. is a neurologist who not only treats his patients’ physical disorders but their love lives as well. Follow along with his case studies on various women with differing backgrounds and personalities, who all have one thing in common - a dire need for romance. One wild night, after spilling secrets about their past failures with the same man they all had been fawning over, the ladies make a bet to see who will marry first. With each episode, Dr. Nat dispenses some sage advice to cure his lovelorn friends’ heart troubles. But it looks like he could use his own advice regarding the guy a dating app listed as his ‘perfect match’! ~~ Adapted from the novel "Wake Up Chani! 'Tuen Thoe Kha! Chani Kha'" (Wake Up ชะนี! Honestly, this might be one of the best series by GMMTV, and definitely my favourite. The things that I really love about this series is definitely the balance between the comical scenes and the serious ones. They did an excellent job with the witty and hilarious comments added on the actors’ lines. The serious scenes are totally gonna impress you with Niti (J’Doc) ‘s words of advice. This series will give you a rollercoaster of emotions, in which you will laugh out loud along with the casts, get pissed, smile like an idiot, and cry. I was really reluctant to watch the last episode as I didn’t want the series to end, but I still did and as a result I cried a lot because I was overwhelmed with all the feelings. I’ve never loved a series this much before. Definitely recommended!
