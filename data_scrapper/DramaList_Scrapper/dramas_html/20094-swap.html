Over the years, Owen's life has been mired in mourning his brother, in pain unable to extricate himself. Until one day, in their business Yuwen Entertainment Company when he encounters the lonely courier, Bare. Bare is not only cute, but he also resembles Owen's brother. His heart can't help but to fall in love with him, he'll keep getting over protective over Bare and do his best to make him happy. Meanwhile, Bare meets a security guard of a building who is also a loner like him, who also keeps protecting him but never stays for too long. The world is filled with billions of people, can all those hearts be filled with love instead of loneliness? Edit Translation

The plot is pretty interesting, however, the acting is not the greatest. It isn't necessarily that the casting was bad, but there were a lot of scenes where the cast didn't do well on the lines and such. In my opinion, it wasn't worth my time. The attractive cast drew me in and kept me watching, but in the end, their appearances weren't enough. I felt Leo (Yuwen You Tian) was the strongest actor as he delivered some heavily emotional scenes, while many of the other actors didn't demonstrate a lot of acting ability. Basically, the biggest problem with this series is the acting. If you can look past that, then definitely check out the first few episodes. If you're like me though, and you can't stand bad acting, don't bother watching this.
