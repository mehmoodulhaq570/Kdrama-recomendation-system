Aihara Mugi can't believe her luck when she reads that the heirs to four major Japanese corporations are searching for their true love on a reality show. Dropping everything, she applies—and shockingly, she’s chosen as one of the three finalists. Along with two other girls, Mugi joins the show, “Marry the Prince,” only to discover it’s not the fairy tale she imagined. The four princes, Riku, Taiga, Terunoshin, and Tatsunari, are more focused on their own drama than romance. But as the show goes on, everyone realizes that love can surprise you when you least expect it. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

It is definitely a great quick show to watch!

I definitely think a lot of people are being a little harsh on the show in the other reviews. This show was very sweet and cute for what it was, as a show that only had 18 episodes that were all 15 minutes long. I definitely think the show was very great, plus the ending was super heartwarming. It was the perfect fairytale ending that most people like. If you’re a avid watcher of J dramas, I definitely think you will like this. It has the amazing J drama charm that makes them unique compared to any other type of asian dramas, definitely would recommend!

story: about 3 common girls who gets into a reality show to be the princesses of 4 prince who are not prince but just really rich dudes. the half of the story was them playing silly with each other the other half with misunderstanding. like any other typical drama which is far from reality. what i did like in this story is the girls friendship. since i thought they would be at each others throat but it was actually the best part of this drama how they become friends. the drama was 18 episodes with maximum 15 minutes i think. the drama cut a lot of information which just gives us a show which is going on in the present but the problems are from the past which didn't even seem like legit problems to begin with. it just made me want to slap the characters in the head and tell them to get their acts straight. anyway i will give the story 3/5

characters: let me just tell i didn't even get why the mc was mc. sure this is a cliche drama but i didn't find anything special in her half of the time she spent shouting and being stupid. only drama guys would think she changed their life and fall in love with her. like heck i don't just get the logic. the characters had character growth out of nowhere because they got each others support which i also don;t get. i just felt their so called problems were not really problems but them just being stupid stubborn airheads and pricks.

to be honest this was supposed to be a light watch and i did enjoy some parts but most parts i wanted to kick them.
