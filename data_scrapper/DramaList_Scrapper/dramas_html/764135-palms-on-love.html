Marshal Xue Dong Feng returns from the front line to attend the funeral of his father, Governor Xue, and take over as warlord. However, he discovers that his fiancée Ji Lian Yun married his father as his concubine five days prior! Xue Dong Feng, believing that the girl he loved betrayed him, was extremely cruel and humiliating to her...and yet he always came to her rescue whenever there was a crisis. While battling his love and hatred for her, he also suffered jealousy as he watched Ji Lian Yun and his sworn brother Xue Zhi Hai get closer and closer. What Xue Dong Feng didn't know was that the reason Ji Lian Yun married into the Governor's Mansion was for revenge. Five years ago, Ji Lian Yun's businessman father was framed in an arms trafficking case and the whole family was convicted, by none other than Dong Feng's father. According to the testimony of Ji Lian Yun's personal maid, Pin'er, it was Xue Dong Feng who killed her parents in prison. Love, hatred, guilt, innocence, truth, and lies...which of these will survive in this tumultuous chaos? (Source: Chinese = Douban || Translation = Jwells at MyDramaList) Edit Translation

Captivating and Intense with a happy ending

This is a mini drama with 10 minute episode duration , its the first one I hv seen which is historical but not ancient...its from the time when there were commanders ruling cities so there are no superpowers but a beautifully designed revenge story of a girl, she is helped by her first love. The chemistry between leads is soo awesome and the entire acting cast has done such a good job. It has an amazing plot which is generally rare in mini dramas.
The drama is soo captivating, it kept me hooked till the end. The visuals and grandeur of the drama makes it one of a kind.
DEFINITELY WORTH WATCHING!! You will love it.

I will always have your time with me in my heart cause that's what keeps me alive

This was a good mini drama and I started it after reading the plot because I found it interesting.
The first 3-4 episodes were really good and that's what makes you want to complete a mini drama.
Mini drama's may also be left half way by viewers if the start is not good but this one ticked that box.
The leads had good chemistry which is one of the main things required in mini dramas.
The supporting characters definitely did a good job in troubling the leads. What was more interesting is that everyone tried to break apart the main leads which made it interesting to see how they will face the challenges that will come up.
There was definitely good amount of push and pull between the leads which will keep the viewers excited about their relation.
I would definitely recommend it for at least a one time watch cause it ticked many boxes that you want to see in a mini drama.
