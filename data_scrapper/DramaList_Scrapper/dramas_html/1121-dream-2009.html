Lee Jang Suk spent some time in a juvenile detention center for stealing wallets from unsuspecting strangers. After he gets out, he meets a sports agent, Nam Jae Il, who's trying to recover from a downfall in his career. Together, they work to achieve their dreams. Jang Suk dreams of becoming a K-1 Champion; and Nam Jae Il dreams of regaining his reputation as a top sports agent. (Source: DramaWiki) Edit Translation

I wasn't sure if I should watch this, because there were many comments like : it's boring or it isnt worth to watch. But I am glad that I watched this.
It's nice drama about achieving your dreams and the long way until you reach the end. It's sometimes boring especially (for me) when they are talking about business. But at all it has good and interesting plot.
About casts. I started to watch this cause Kim Bum but after first ep there will be really nice boys with perfect bodies. Ahh and the matches were really good, you can feel that intense between them.
About music. Title song is Countdown by SHINee and also other songs are good.
IF you have time and want to watch drama in which you dont have to think about love and devil parents this is your choice :)

I absolutely don't regret watching Dream. It was one of the best dramas I ever watched^^ The topic follow-your-dream may be quite a cliche but still the drama is amazing, and I watched in in less than 2 days!

Story 8/10
The story starts very slow but paces up really really quickly...I was hooked in episode 3 (which is less than with some better ranked dramas on MDL) and then I couldn't stop watching...
I loved the characters and the development of the story...it's definitely the best drama I ever saw about achieving your dream...

Cast 10/10
Although I thought I would only like Kim Bum, I loved the whole cast!! They all worked so well together and I could really feel with them...sometimes, I admit, I wished for more reactions, but it's not that often...

Music 10/10
I LOVED the music! It was in the right scenes and I couldn't get enough of it!!! Especially SHINee - Countdown was great (and somehow a little bit different from the usual main songs of OSTs^^)

Review 9/10
Oh, I'm definitely going to rewatch this. First drama ever to really earn the 9 points for it (I actually don't know if I ever gave 9 points...).

Overall 10/10

Note: With this drama you're going to cry, laugh and then tremble of excitement^^ at least I did :D
