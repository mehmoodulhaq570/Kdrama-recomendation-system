The cast members of 'The Penthouse' wrap up the first season of the riveting drama while Shin Dong Yeop and Jang Do Yeon moderate with questions and popular theories from viewers. Each of the actors discusses the characters they portrayed and how the director approached the program's suspenseful content while watching behind-the-scenes footage of the drama’s shoots. Clues and previews for the upcoming second season are also presented. (Source: MyDramaList, SBS, Zapzee) Edit Translation

watch this interview only after you have watched all 3 seasons of Penthouse

●I watched Penthouse when it finished and damn it was HeadBanging. But I found this interview now and am glad I found it now. My illusion of seeing them as bad characters would break cause all the actors are friendly among themselves so it would be a bummer if I watched this while watching Penthouse. All the actors are really different from the characters they portrayed. So I would suggest watching this if you have already watched the 3 seasons.
If you haven't watched this interview might ruin the tension, anxiety, and pain you have toward the drama.

●The Interview was all about discussing characters, and popular scenes in a fun way and the hardship they faced up during shooting. Its also revealed how they were curious about what was going to happen in the next episode. I was a bit sad that the Youngsters were not involved in the interview as much as the adults, so maybe the next season's Interview.

●Felt like adding this from the discussion cause the way it makes sense.
Why Penthouse was a massive success?
Park Eun Seok - cause Not everyone just toiled hard but also because everyone was a great lead role and also an amazing supporting character. I think thats what brought the drama great success. It felt like the main character changed with every episode and they all have a solid storyline. They were all main characters and sub-character. There were no plot holes.
