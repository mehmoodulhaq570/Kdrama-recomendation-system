After discovering her boyfriend Pong's infidelity and his impending marriage to Vithinee, Bow returns to Thailand. Heartbroken, she goes to a club, where she's nearly drugged by a criminal but is saved by Tos, Vithinee's protective older brother. Tos, eager to prevent Bow from ruining his sister's happiness, claims to have slept with her, forcing Bow's father to marry her off to him. I’ll start by saying that if you enjoy the original you will enjoy this one. I have now officially seen them both. I was a little hesitant to watch this one as I feel somewhat strange about the first one but I decided to give it a shot hoping that it would take a different approach however it did not. The positives; the cast did an amazing job portraying the characters who they were intended to be. Their emotions were evolved very well and it was very convincing. The supporting cast were good as well. I have nothing negative to say about the acting, the wardrobe or the quality of film however the plot itself was a bit disturbing. I felt the same about the first one. To summarize they have essentially turned a victim into a spouse.

I have watched a similar series but for some reason I didn’t get the same vibes like I got with this one. This one was a little eerie. Please don’t read below if you don’t want this boiler. What we have is our female lead who went away for a while and came back to discover that her boyfriend had not only cheated on her but had also gotten married. He was very apologetic and continue to make attempts to get her back. Once the wife’s brother finds out about it he then lies to the girls parents that he defiled her and that he loved her and wanted to marry her just so he could make sure she was unavailable so his sisters husband wouldn’t go back to work. This twisted part is that the parents dumped her (her being unaware) off in the middle of nowhere with this guy and left her there. He essentially harassed her and bothered her to the point that he started to like her. As a viewers I assume that we were supposed to empathize with him and hope that they would end up well. As a female I was again, Disgusted by the fact that the writers thought it was sweet for her to fall in love with somebody that lied on her, harassed, stalked her and caused her family to look down on her for his own reasons. To make matters worse she had no idea that he was her ex boyfriends brother-in-law. Other events take place and he pretty much kidnapped her and tortures her. Long story short they love each other again and live happily ever after.

This is the most twisted crap I’ve ever seen. Not only was the male leads behaviors equivalent to that of a psycho, her parents willingness to hand her over was even more disgusting. I did not find this romantic but I did find it creepy and somewhat disturbing. I don’t care how many fields of flowers you walk in the whole plot and was sick and unromantic. I gave it a 6 for the acting.

I just accidentally found this one, and the first episode intrigued me so I decided to watch the old version (NOTE: THIS DRAMA IS A REMAKE OF 2009 WWH). Im not saying you should watch the old one to understand this but trust me watching the old version will make you compare everything and appreciate that Porshe'Tos and Bow'Now really outdone the main characters. Anyway, what Im trying to say (im being nonsense lol) is this version is really really good y'all!

I don't wanna spoil you lol but lemme just tell you more about this drama if you want to read go on. LMAO

It all started with Bow got cheated by her ex bf Pong and Tos who will do anything because he love his one and only lil sister which is Pong's wife. They end up being a husband and wife (tho didn't sign any marriage license) Because Tos wants to take Bow away from Pong so he will not dare leave Nee (Pong's wife and his sister). He and Bow's parents forced her to live in a vineyard that Tos owned. I feel bad for Bow because she was forced to live with a stranger she doesn't love at all with ulterior motive (which of course she does not know) while trying to cope up with her heartbreak. You'll get to see how Tos keeps on teasing our FL by calling her teerak/babe which I really do love. The way they bicker and some sweet scenes. And of course how they eventually end up loving each other (I think Tos already like Bow the first time he saw her) and of course how Bow will soften up to our ML.
I guess that's it? The rest you find out! Did i spoil anything?!

Casts:
*Porshe as Tos- (the old one is a naaah tho i love Wier) in this version, he is smart, sweet and playful to fl, Porshe portrayal is so satisfying, and also the way he didn't want to entertain our nang'rai's love even though he had this time that he is mad at fl, is what I really do like the most. You'll might hate him in the climax part like for 2 episodes, but I think for me he is the character that you want to hate but you just can't because he won your heart throughout the drama. And of course, this is lakorn, we always have this moment to hate pra'eks because sometimes they are stupid.
*Now as Bow - yo girl is gorgeous, choosing Now as Bow is the best thing ch7, kantana or whoever he/she did. Why? She really outdone Bow's role (again if you watched the old version you'll know the big diff of BowPinky and BowNow) Bow in this version is smart (yaaasss we got a smart couple my teerak babies),and a fighter. She can kick ass. I really do admire her. Oh! She can be cute sometimes, I think that's Now's charm.

Those two are my 'teerak' /honey/babe couple. I love the way they bicker and call each other teerak, khun samee (Bow to Tos). Even when they act sweet in front of others lol

*Film as Baithong - girl is so insane! The previous version is crazy but this one is intense, Film really did a great job portraying an obsessed, psychotic character.
