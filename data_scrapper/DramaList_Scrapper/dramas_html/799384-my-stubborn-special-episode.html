I love BoatOat. They bring some serious heat.

The storyline is simple and straightforward, it's about two people madly in love who express thier feelings through phyiscal affection.

I loved the series and the special episodes. To me the plot was thier relationship and I'm fine with that. I actually enjoyed a series that focused on the M/M leads relationship. More Please!

Instead of intimacy being the big ending its part of the story, which is very realistic when supported with all the sweet loving scenes that were just adorable, fluffy, and cute.

Boat and Oat are an amazing M/M couple. Thier chemistry is off the charts and thier acting is really good. They make thier relationship feel real, with so much emotion and passion.

The kissing scenes are so beautiful and believable, they actually look like two people in love. Not the dead fish kissing or the grimace expression so many BL stars make before they kiss.

Boat and Oat are actors not porn stars, so what they are doing is hard and they deserve credit. Most actors cannot do NC scenes like these two. It takes an enormous amount of confidence and trust to show this level of intimacy.

There are several people in the room during filming and they have to do so many shots to get the scene right. I've read that NC scenes are extremely exhausting and very labor intensive.
