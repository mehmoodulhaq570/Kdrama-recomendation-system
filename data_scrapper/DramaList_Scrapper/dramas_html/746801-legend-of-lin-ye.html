This was like a gift in a time where bromance and BL was banned in China.

After watching almost all the bromance available in China, this was the next thing that kept me glued and entertained. It had the Shizun X Disciple elements, which I sometimes have a hate and love relationship with but this one did it well. It kind of reminds you of the Chinese novel SVSS which is a BL, it feels like a life adaptation of the novel. Anyways, based on acting the cast did a good job and the storyline was very addicted as it's in short episodes which is easy to watch (I binged watched it in two days) but the second season...it was good but I won't spoil it though, the ending felt kind of rushed out and confusing at the same time, though I highly recommend it.

Definitely Added to my BL Favorites

I LOVE IT!!! and would very much want to have another sequel! The 3 minutes episodes highlighted each scene in the story and made the watching time really quick, the actors portrayed their characters nicely and made quite an undeniable chemistry (also they're very good-looking). I would also like to commend the fight scenes which was very good and has the right amount of intensity to it, the visual effects and music was just fine, setting was also alright, wardrobe is okay, overall... for me, it's good.
Now, on the other side, the shortness of the episodes kind of deducted some points on the story, the small count of casts are also noticeable, scene change isn't that smooth, shot framing are sometimes not appropriate and few more others. It's all fine for me though... I'll surely rewatch this over and over again.
