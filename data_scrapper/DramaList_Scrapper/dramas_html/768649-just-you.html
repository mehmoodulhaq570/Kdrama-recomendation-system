This one is recommended!!!

Honestly, even though Her Trajection felt a lot like Marry My Husband, I absolutely loved the remake. The similarities in the plot-line were undeniable especially the theme of second chances and revenge—but the Chinese version brought its own unique charm that made it stand out. It wasn’t just a copy; it was a reimagining that gave more emotional depth and visual appeal.

The pacing of the story was tight, and despite being a short drama, every episode felt meaningful and impactful. What really impressed me was how the characters were developed so well in such a short span. The female lead was powerful yet vulnerable, and the chemistry between the leads felt natural and intense. I was fully drawn into her journey of pain, growth, and self-empowerment.

The acting was another highlight. Each actor perfectly embodied their roles, especially the protagonist, who delivered her performance with so much emotion and nuance. The antagonist, too, was convincing without being overly dramatic. The cinematography, the music, and the set design added to the immersive experience—it felt polished and cinematic, not rushed.

Her Trajection might be short, but it’s definitely worthy of attention. It proves that a drama doesn’t need 30+ episodes to tell a powerful story. The emotional beats hit hard, and the themes of love, betrayal, and personal strength were portrayed beautifully.

I really hope we get more gems like this in the future—unique remakes that respect the original but still bring something new to the table. If you enjoy dramas that are both touching and empowering, Her Trajection is definitely worth watching.

Wait is this Marry My Husband Again?

If you haven’t seen the 2024 drama “Marry My Husband” then just watch this because it is a much shorter version of that. Trust me this drama does not stray that far away from the 2024 version.

You have the “king of the mini dramas” Dai Gao Zheng as the male lead. His role is in comparison to the male lead of Marry My Husband, Na In Woo. Same demeanor, reserved, laid back, but wanting to be the protector of Shu Yu. Be prepared to have no kissing from him until episode 16. There are only 31 episodes. Yes, we do get to see those famous abs.

The female lead Li Mo Zhi, you will recognize her from “My Lethal Man.” She is exactly how Park Min Young was in Marry My husband too. She was very dowdy, obedient to her husband and mother-in-law, and was sick.

Again, if you saw “Marry My Husband” you will not be surprised at anything that I will tell you with regards to this story. So, let start.

The FL (Li Mo Zhi aka Shu Yan) is killed at the hands of her husband (Luo Chu Fan aka Han Tian Yu) while his lover Ye Wei and bestie to Shu Yan watched. She came home one day to find him and Ye Wei on the steps half naked and discussing how she didn’t have long to live. Han Tian Yu had taken an insurance policy of 80 million out on her. He stopped her from getting the best treatment knowing that her father had died from cancer. He thought his chances of not treating her would push her to die sooner.

How did she die? Just the same way in the 2024 version. It was a heated discussion once Shu Yan busted the lovers, so much so until Han Tian Yu pushed her into the glass table killing her. Sound familiar?

As Shu Yan laid in her own pool of blood, she thought how unfair it was for to die that way. Opening her eyes, she sees she is alive and standing beside Han Tian Yu. Her husband that just killed her. Looking at the billboard with Shen Wei Zhou face on it stated, “Valentine’s Day 2020”. How did she return back five years. Her memory of Han Tian Yu killing her was still fresh in her mind and she begins to attack him. There fight spills onto the stage where ML (Dai Gao Zheng aka Shen Wei Zhou) is performing. Nearly falling off the stage, Shen Wei Zhou catches her and realizes it is Shu Yan.

We flashback to 2025 and see the funeral of Shu Yan. Shen Wei Zhou attending the funeral bringing flowers. Standing in front of her headstone, he was filled with not only remorse, but regret that he could not protect her.

The connection between Shu Yan and Shen Wei Zhou stems from their university days. She was in a drunken stupor when she saw a bunch of boys bullying him. She intervened by yelling and as she was yelling a police siren went off. Shen Wei Zhou thanked her. Seeing how drunk she was, he hung out with her the whole night. Shu Yan never remembered him or the night. Shen Wei Zhou continued to follow her after that night. He found that she liked a cat at the campus and built a small home for him. They continued this until Shen Wei Zhou got injured. He could not leave the hospital for a month. After leaving the hospital, he went back to where the cat was, hoping to see Shu Yan, but she was gone. He kept the cat which he named Bai. He finally sees her again after he joins the same company she was currently employed, only to find he is too late with possibly having a relationship when he sees her with Han Tian Yu having lunch and discovers they are dating.

Flashing back to 2025 we see Shen Wei Zhou is hit by an SUV crossing the street and he dies. He is sent back to 2020. We never get any further information as to what caused him to go back and why he returned. Was it his regret with not protecting her that he was sent back? They don't say.

We also have Shu Yan realizing once she "died" how much Ye Wei wanted to have everything that was hers. Well-armed this time Shu Yan was able to take Ye Wei head on. The best comeuppance on Ye Wei was when she wanted Shu Yan to go to their class reunion dressed in the same outfit along with the shoes. Shu Yan gets a complete makeover from head to toe with the help of Shen Jiao who was the sister of Shen Wei Zhou. She stunned everyone at the reunion with her look including shocking Ye Wi. Shu Yan explains everything that Ye Wei had done. Even calling Han Tian Yu to confirm that they were dating.

They still did not believe her and accused her of stealing Chu Chen. Enter Chu Chen to the reunion. The class bullies realize that everything that Ye Wei said from back in school was a lie even down to “stealing” Chu Chen from her. Shu Yan doesn’t find out until after that not only did Shen Wei Zhou finds Chu Chen but had him show up at the reunion to drive the nail in the coffin for Ye Wei. Oh, and those bullies got her good.

Not without his own issues, Shen Wei Zhou was engaged to Meng Ya. This girl is a piece of work. They had been engaged for three years. Meng Ya never paid any attention to him until he tells his grandfather that he wants the engagement cancelled. Meng Ya hears there is a female that he likes in the picture. Talk about coming back asap. She never allowed someone to take something of hers. Thinking that she can have her way, she goes to Shen Wei Zhou’s house unannounced trying to put the moves on him. He puts her in a choke hold and throws her out. Really good move on his part. His only issue is his grandfather who is siding with Meng Ya due to a family promise.

Throughout the drama we have Shu Yan trying to change her fate with Han Tian Yu by pushing him into the arms of Ye Wei. Shen Wei Zhou is trying to make sure that nothing happens to her when he is not around. He takes his protection of her a step further by teaching her judo. You will like this too. I don’t know how he kept a straight face with her jumping on him all the time. Now, that is heaven right there.

You also get to see Shen Wei Zhou’s jealous side with Chu Chen and Han Tian Yu. He couldn’t stand Han Tian Yu, and all those moments are funny especially when you he had him getting doused with water over and over again. Hilarious. Han Tian Yu was too mad.

They do finally realize they both were sent back when Shu Yan mentions He Ju. He plays a major key two-fold because he is the founder of Douli Wemedia platform that was a company that skyrocketed in three years. On one hand helping Shu Yan get money and on the other hand giving Shen Wei Zhou the upper hand with his grandfather by letting him know about He Ju pushing the idea came for Shu Yan. This he feels will be the catalyst to break the engagement with Meng Ya.

She finally breaks free of Han Tian Yu when she dresses so obnoxious when meeting his parents. They loathe her. Confronting him the next day at work, everything is put on the table, and she throws the ring at him making sure that everyone knew he cheated on her with Ye Wei.

They do get married; find out about Shen Wei Zhou and how much money she had. Ye Wei calls in Shu Yan’s mother to try to blackmail her which fails. The three of them, Meng Ya, Ye Wei, Han Tian Yu all decide to band together and kill Shu Yan. This was to get all the money that Shen Wei Zhou left to her which if she died would go to her mother. Yes, Shu Yan’s mother is involved too.

Shen Wei Zhou knowing what was going to transpire with all of them trying to kill Shu Yan. He races to the scene and just as the truck comes down, Shen Wei Zhou is hit. This is the opposite of what happened in Marry My Husband. He stopped her from being killed by putting his vehicle in front and he was hit and was then in a coma. Shen Wei Zhou doesn’t stay in the hospital long.

To end all of this for the last time. Shen Wei Zhou and Shu Yan set up Han Tain Yu and Ye Wei. Of course, everything fails miserably. Ye Wei kills Hai Tain Yu. She goes over to Shu Yan's house to burn the house down with her in it. Being prepared, Shu Yan tells her that everything was recorded and to turn herself in. She is arrested by the police. In jail, Ye Wei has pretty much snapped and is calling out Shu Yan's name.

Meng Ya, ex-fiancée to Shen Wei Zhou is trying to get out of dodge really quick. Shen Wei Zhou's grandfather took all of her shares and has decided to ship her off to Africa to do volunteer work. She wasn't having that. She tells her closest bodyguard to get her a ticket to the States. He tells her to turn herself in and that is when she finds out he has been working for Shen Wei Zhou. Hearing the police siren and not wanting to be caught she runs out of the house, not looking as she was running across the street and gets hit by a car and yes dies.

Shu Yan and Shen Wei Zhou go to the cemetery to visit her father where he proposes to her. Yes, they get married.

Fate has been changed.

Again, it is enjoyable, but if you saw Marry My Husband, well you get the gist.
