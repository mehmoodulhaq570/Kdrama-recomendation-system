This drama tells the story of Mo Su Qing, a seemingly weak orphan girl in trouble, who approaches Ye Zhong Jue, the president of the group, to avenge her family, and sets up a scheme step by step. It never occurred to her that the smooth progress of all plans was due to the many routines set up by Ye Zhong Jue. The little white rabbit VS the big bad wolf: both sides claim to control the overall situation, but they don't know that there is no hunter in this love game, both of them are prey... Cliched average romance

If you wanted to start this drama, you got have a reason. My reason was LGY and i came for him.
The trailer looked dope and everything about this drama looked intriguing but honestly, there was nothing interesting about this drama because it just an average revenge/ romance story.
We have a rich girl and rich boy. Girl is forced to marry a manwh*re so she devises a plan to get the ML while ML wants to have her cause of some childhood treasure memory. Falling in each others traps (and laps), there dont want to get out and the story continues down the cliche route. We see the normal "i will do anything for you" ML and "I am on my own and nobody to love me" FL.
The acceptable reason to still watch this drama while being heavily cliched is cause its short. The scenes switch fast. You can finish this drama within three hours and the best part is even if you skip (episodes), you still understand.
Ofc, the cast was lit. The FL was very weird to be in the beginning but she grew on me. There was also alot of cast for this small drama tho everyone did their part to fit in their role. The music was non-exisent. Even if it was there, it wasnt that noticable.
This is the drama for you if you just want to finish a small drama to finish your challenge or you are a new watcher. I wouldnt say this was epic but for whatever reason you came for, its easier to stay thinking abt it.
