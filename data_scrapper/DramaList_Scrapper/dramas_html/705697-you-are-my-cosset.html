As manager of a dessert shop, Cheng Mu has only one desire: to make his shop the best in the business. Working hard to make that dream come true, Cheng Mu is overjoyed when his shop begins to grow in popularity. But with that popularity comes the attention of those he would rather avoid, namely snobby food bloggers who think more of themselves than the food they’re reviewing. Among the most popular food bloggers is Jiang Si Han, a wealthy food blogger with a dedicated following. Following the rumors surrounding Cheng Mu’s shop, Si Han arrives to find out for herself what all of the fuss is about. Skeptical at first, Cheng Mu’s delicious desserts eventually win Si Han over. Of course, having a kid as cute as Rui Rui around to tear down Si Han’s defenses only made winning her over that much easier. Finding herself returning to Cheng Mu’s shop more and more frequently, Si Han can’t deny the feelings that stir, every time she’s there. With a heart growing more fond of Cheng Mu and Rui Rui by the day, will Si Han find her life taking a rather unexpected turn? (Source: Viki) Edit Translation

A drunken kiss that sealed my heart

To be fair , it didnt wow me , but it's a decent watch ! It's only a 16 episode drama, so something felt rushed.

FL decides to confess to her class monitor that she has a crush on him but she was drunk when that happened so it turned out to be the ML , and she ends up kissing him. one year later , they meet again however female lead has no recollection of male lead. FL is a food blogger who likes desserts. ML Is a dessert chef. FL wants to learn more about the chef's desserts.

Romance ( ML +FL ) some chemistry but not passionate enough, to be honest the 2ML, 2FL had more passion and stole more of the moments/limelight . the ML+FL is more cute, sweet, simple, while 2ML + 2FML relationship was more lust and passion driven. I think because of the 16 ep, there was not enough momentum to build up their relationship.

What i liked-
Desserts- looked super yummy and i like that fact that each dessert told a story of what is happening with the leads at the moment.
The kid - he is a cute factor and liked that he try to step the aunt aka FL AND godfather aka ML together. They are cute as a little family but the time together was too little and he eventually got sent away and came back for the last ep...



Things to note about this drama that is not obvious
1) The male lead has/ has had depression... but its not very obvious ( unless i missed something) They revealed it more towards the middle/end . The male lead doesnt tell the female lead. The male lead gets triggered when he hears the word " lei zui" meaning burden.. For all the times that was happening.. i was expecting the female lead to discover him and comfort him but that doesnt happen...

What i didnt like
Livestreamer bullying : towards the end there is someone who tries to sabotage and ruin the reputation of the ML dessert store. Rude livestreamers try to see for themselves and not even care about what the owners and employees have to day, despite rejecting them saying they cant come in. ( felt like i would of given the drama a higher score if not for that last random incident)
