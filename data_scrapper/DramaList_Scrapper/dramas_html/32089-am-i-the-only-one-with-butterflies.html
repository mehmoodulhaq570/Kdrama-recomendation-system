A very short but also very cute GL drama! The
office setting was nice and the female leads
have a great chemistry together. There were
many sweet moments, which made my heart
flutter and even I was feeling butterflies in
my stomach hehe. The story is about Jung
Ah, who on the first day of work falls head
over heels for her manager Lee Ji Won. Her
daydreaming about her during work hours
was just so cute! The story ended with a big
cliffhanger but luckily there is a 2nd season.

I rate Am I The Only One with Butterflies? 9/10.
