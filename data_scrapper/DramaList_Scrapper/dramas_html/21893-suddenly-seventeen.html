Back to the 17-year-old high school campus, Liang Xia and Mao Liang were 28 and after facing a discussion they found themselves again 10 years back, thus facing again the life on campus out of the blue. Liang Xia wanted to live as an adolescent "no worries" and Mao Liang's "adult thinking", but even so the two frequently made happy moments. In the struggle after the ridiculous contradiction, they were determined to change the fate of people around to help everyone find a positive direction in life. You might say its typical like time travel amd stuff but this drama is one of the cutest time travel dramas I've watch. I like how the story do not mainly focus on time traveling, like what, how happened and stuff but focus on changing the life they had with the chance. I also like how they dont focus mainly on the two ML's, about their time travel, but also to the other support characters. The plot was justified all through out the series, though the rundown of some events are a little bit slow or fast idk.

The cast are amazing and justify each of the characters, I really commend, FL's acting, unlike the usual female leads on the dramas I've watched, shes someone who stand out for me. Jiang Chao is charismatic, esp. when he smile dang!(´∀｀)♡

The ost fits the drama and added more energy to it.

Actually Im planning on rewatching it, IM TELLING YOU, THIS ONES A CUTE DRAMA CATCH, HIGHLY RECOMMENDED!!! WORTH THE WATCH, ESP. FOR YOUNGSTERS, YOU'LL EMBRACE YOUR YOUTH MORE!^ω^*thumbs up!*

Underrated but worth watching

Time travel is not my thing yet i decided to give this a try and am glad that i did it.
The cast was pretty good. Acting was on point. It is a very beautiful story about youth, friendship and love.
It had 26 episodes which were not that long. Am glad that it was not dragged till 30 or 36 episodes so it did not become boring at the end.
The love between the male leads was too precious and beautiful. All the characters were beautifully portrayed by all the actors
The realisation and the way you think when you are 28 and when you are 17 are total different but the age 17 is really warm and precious. This drama portrays these realisation and feelings.
