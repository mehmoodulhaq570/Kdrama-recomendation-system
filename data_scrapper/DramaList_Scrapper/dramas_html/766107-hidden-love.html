Effective Rom-revenge series (good results compared to the budget :)

PLOT: The FL (architect mother killed and father in prison, then mad) joins forces with the ML, the illegitimate son of a powerful (and cruel) tycoon, to take revenge on this tycoon. However, the truth behind the events of 15 years ago is more complex than it seems...

+++ CL with intense tension and interactions and an excellent chemistry : Sun Jia Qi & Wanyan Luo Rong,
+++ Excellent OSTs
+++ Fast-paced production, without any downtime, with beautiful framing ( areally good job is done on light and frame)
+++ Well-written, coherent story.

### Low-budget series (few costumes, few sets) but even the photomontages are of poor quality :( :( :(
### Why in dramas does the director consider the scene where the FL shaves (with an antique razor) the ML who doesn't have a hair to shave to be very romantic????
### A few plot holes and obvious coincidences, but it's okay.

=> Excellent performances from the actors that make up for the series' flaws.

Kill Lover is a nice revenge romance drama with Wanyan Luo Rong as Qi Dong and Sun Jia Qi as You You/Qi Ge Ge in the leading couple’s role. The cast did a good job bringing their characters to life and the drama has great cinematography and editing, and very nice OST. The leading couple has very good chemistry but, sometimes, their facial expressions are a bit bland. The romance is nice and the leading couple has some good interactions and a few nicely done romantic scenes. There are minor issues with the drama’s with story’s fluidity but, the drama is easy to follow. Overall. this is a nice and entertaining drama, enjoy it!
