Shy and socially anxious Yun Li enrolls in graduate school to prove her independence and unexpectedly reunites with her high school crush, the brilliant Fu Shi Ze. Determined to win his heart, she steps out of her comfort zone and pursues him. Their love flourishes, but past traumas and personal differences drive them apart. Years later, Fu Shi Ze, now a renowned researcher, is determined to win Yun Li back when she returns from abroad. Through growth and perseverance, they find their way back to each other. (Source: TMDB; edited by MyDramaList) ~~ Adapted from the web novel "Folding Moon" (折月亮) by Zhu Yi (竹已). Edit Translation
