Years ago, Mo Ning Xi lost everything -- her grandfather, a happy marriage, her infant son -- when she was kicked out of the house by her mother-in-law after the manipulative Song Jia Yin fabricated evidence to make it appear as though Mo Ning Xi was having an affair. Already pregnant with twins, she moved abroad where she became a reknown fashion designer. She returns six years later, ready to reclaim everything that had been taken from her, while also investigating her grandfather's untimely death and dealing with Song Jia Yin's renewed attempts to get rid of her. She is aided by her ex-husband, Li Xi Cheng, the handsome and wealthy CEO of the Li Group, who never stopped loving her. (Source: MyDramaList) Edit Translation

A quick summary:
Mo Ning Xi's grandfather passed away and then she was kicked out of the house by her mother in law and went abroad. Six years later, she came back as a well-known fashion designer with her two kids to make things right.
She met her ex-husband again and the story goes on.

The cast is good looking, The ML is handsome and FL is also pretty but had an odd eye make-up that I couldn't get used to until the end.
The acting overall is okay and the leads have chemistry.
The ost is nice.

There is one thing bothered me while watching this drama! That it had so many slow scenes.
If you could turn a blind eye on that, and some incoherent dubbing, you might enjoy watching this drama more than I did.

Anyways ML carried the show for me. I don't dislike the FL but she didn't do much of anything by herself.

The story is quite predictable and has lots of cliche, So I think it's just an okay watch to pass time.
I might re-watch it in future.

Cute Mini - But Choppy

The ML is good. Looking forward to seeing him in more dramas. The FL was okay; however, between makeup, hair or delayed acting it became distracting to watch. The OST was okay. Even the story concept was okay. The description does not match what is being played. The pacing was pretty good. However, I would have preferred really understanding and seeing the textile everyone wanted on full display besides the various board meeting scenes. The villain and her 'helper' became laughable seeing that they had overwhelming accessibility to the main leads even after being discovered time and time again.
